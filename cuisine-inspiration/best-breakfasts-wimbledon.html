Wimbledon’s best breakfasts

  • 26 June 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 4 min
breakfasts wimbledon waffles avocado
Share this article

Anyone for tennis? The Championships at Wimbledon have come around already. So whether you're pitching at Murray Mound for the tournament, or just live nearby, we've got some good news for you. We'll be delivering Wimbledon's best breakfasts, from 3rd to 16th July, to prep you for those long days watching the tennis. Everyday throughout The Championships, you can get great Deliveroo breakfasts from 8am. So check out what's on the menu and get ordering.

Carluccio's

A post shared by Carluccio's (@carluccios) on May 28, 2017 at 1:46am PDT

Free range eggs, smoky pancetta, sausage, sautéed mushrooms, tomato and toasted Italian bread, served all day? You cannot be serious! Oh we are. Carluccio's Breakfast is perfect for those days when you want to take it easy in the morning before a full day of fast and furious tennis.

Bill's

A post shared by Bill's Restaurant (@billsrestaurant) on May 15, 2017 at 2:55am PDT

Soft, fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes from Bill's, topped with banana, strawberry, blueberries and their signature syrup, are your go-to brekkie when you're watching the Wimbledon action from your sofa. So if you can't make it to Centre Court this year, never mind, because pancakes fix everything.

CAU

If a Wimbledon weekend is on the cards, don't miss any of the action – get breakfast in bright and early. Coal Roasted Peaches, perched on top of natural yoghurt and granola from CAU will get you prepped for a trip out courtside.

Le Pain Quotidien

A post shared by Le Pain Quotidien UK (@lepainquotidienuk) on Mar 11, 2017 at 3:33am PST

Avocado, scrambled eggs and smoked salmon – that's game, set, match to Le Pain Quotidien. This winning combination for breakfast is light enough for a hot day out at the courts, but packed with plenty of protein to see you through a nail-biting Singles Final.

Mr Pretzels

Need to beat the crowds heading to Murray Mound, but don't want to miss breakfast? Then you need something to grab and go that you can dish out to your mates. Think a five-pack of sweet and savoury pretzels from Mr Pretzels before making a dash for a prime spot in the sunshine.

Lu-Ma Café

A post shared by Lu-Ma (@lumacafe) on Apr 9, 2017 at 1:18am PDT

Has tennis season inspired you to dust off your racket and sweatbands? Even if you can't play like a pro yet, you can still eat like one, with a Vegan Protein Brekkie from Lu-Ma Café. This mother and daughter-run restaurant is all about eating clean, so you'll find nothing but goodies in this breakfast that's bound to get you going for a game of doubles.

Waffle Jack's

A Breakfast Waffle from Waffle Jack's is a grand slam of a breakfast. This 7" waffle – topped with bacon, cheese and maple syrup – ticks both boxes for the morning after your favourite has been knocked out of the competition, or as a celebratory brekkie after a big win the day before.

Wafflemeister

A post shared by Wafflemeister UK (@wafflemeister) on Feb 8, 2017 at 8:06am PST

If you can't get your strawberries and cream in the stands at this year's Championships then why not get them over a hot waffle from Wafflemeister instead? The Strawberry Heaven is topped with squiggles of rich Belgian chocolate – in milk or dark – for a doubly decadent breakfast.

Pret A Manger

A post shared by Pret (@pretamangeruk) on Sep 4, 2014 at 11:45pm PDT

Everyone's got a favourite breakfast from Pret, right? And you go straight to it every morning on your way to work. Well now you can get it delivered throughout The Championships. There's no better way to start a sunny day out at the tennis than with some freshly baked croissants, or a delicious fruity yoghurt pot to get you going.

First service! Deliveroo will be delivering breakfasts in Wimbledon from 3rd July to 16th July 2017, from 8am, so you can enjoy the action from home, or grab something delicious before heading courtside.

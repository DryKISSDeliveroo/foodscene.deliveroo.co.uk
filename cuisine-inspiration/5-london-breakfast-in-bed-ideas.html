5 London breakfasts worth staying in bed for

  • 16 March 2016
  • Lizzie Graham
  • Read time: 4 min
breakfast brunch london
Share this article

Why would you ruin a weekend lie-in by doing the breakfast run? Or even getting out of bed for that matter? We've lined up some of the very best brekkies-in-beds to suit every mood.

So, wrap up in your duvet, hit snooze and wait to be woken up the right way with some morning-making London breakfasts delivered to your door.

1. "The Hangover" Breakfast in Bed – La Brasserie's Full English

So, last night got a bit out of hand, and leaving the comfort of your duvet is unthinkable. The only thing is, you're mad hungry and you need a full English pronto! Keep cuddling that duvet and order yourself the full works from La Brasserie, which includes two eggs, bacon, sausage, portobello mushroom and tomato. Bit messy maybe, but so worth it…

Get breakfast delivered to: beds in South Kensington.

2. "The Wholesome" Breakfast in Bed – The Good Life Eatery's Goodness Bowls

Good Life Eatery is your go-to menu for nourishing, healthy and substantial brekkie options. Have a peruse of their Goodness Bowls for duvet-friendly scoffing, such as this Acai Bowl with homemade granola, Acai puree, strawberries, banana, blueberry, hemp seeds and bee pollen.

Get breakfast delivered to: beds in Chelsea.

3. "The Romantic" Breakfast in Bed – Snaps & Rye's Kedgeree

Two spoons and a big bowl of Snaps & Rye's creamy and delicious kedgeree – what could be more romantic than that? London's only Danish restaurant does the most hygge (meaning cosy in Danish) brekkie-in-bed, with this lovely smoked haddock, cured salmon, tomato, spinach and rice creation.

Get breakfast delivered to: beds in Notting Hill.

4. "The (Friendly) Sleepover" Breakfast in Bed – The Henry Root's Eggs Benedict

You've had a surprisingly peaceful night's sleep top-to-toeing with your best mate, and now you want to eat brekkie and watch weird cat videos on the internet. Keep the comradeship strong by ordering scrummy Eggs Benedict from classy-yet-casual dining room, The Henry Root. Hollandaise sauce in bed, mmmm.

Get breakfast delivered to: Chelsea

5. "The Kickstarter" Breakfast in Bed – Down To Earth's Full English

Prevent any chance of getting out of bed on the wrong side by taking a bit more quality bed-time, and order yourself Down To Earth's vibrant take on a Full English. Just listen to this – Tamari and garlic marinated mushrooms, roasted cherry tomatoes, scrambled eggs with chives, homemade Mexican-style beans, seasonal greens and toasted sourdough. Now, doesn't sound like a good day in the making?

Get breakfast delivered to: Holland Park

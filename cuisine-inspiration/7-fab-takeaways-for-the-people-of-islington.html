7 fab takeaways for the people of Islington

  • 18 June 2017
  • Editor
  • Read time: 4 min
islington burger pizza cake curry tandoori london
Share this article

Islington has everything you could wish for when it comes to food. But with so much on offer, making a choice on where to order from is half the battle. To help you out, we've rounded up a few places that should give you inspiration for your next takeaway.  

1. Tandoori Chicken, Delhi Grill

A post shared by Delhi Grill (@delhi_grill) on May 14, 2017 at 7:35am PDT

If it's Indian cuisine that takes your fancy, look no further. The Delhi Grill is the perfect choice for authentic Indian goodies, offering up a range of wraps, grills and home cooked curries. We love the Tandoori Chicken they whip up here – freshly grilled in herbs and spices, it really packs a punch. If you like a bit of everything though, opt for the mixed grill, where you'll get tandoori chicken, sheekh kebabs and zingy spiced lamb chops.

 Where: Delhi Grill, Islington

2. The Flameboy Burger, Gone Burger

A post shared by GoneBurger (@goneburger_uk) on Nov 11, 2016 at 10:29am PST

Time to talk burgers. Gone Burger is any burger fan's heaven, with a mouth-watering selection of patty packed wonders. It's hard to pick a favourite here, but we love the Flameboy. A potato and chickpea fritter is topped with coconut sauce, fresh pineapple, chilli and red onion. And this delight is totally vegetarian and vegan friendly too. Served on a soft bun and smothered in Gone Burger's signature topping sauce, this burger is definitely one worth trying.

Where: Gone Burger, Islington

3. Bufalina pizza, Oi Vita Pizzeria

Is there anything better than a traditional stone baked pizza from an Italian pizzeria? And that's exactly what you get over at Oi Vita – these guys have just about everything you could ask for when it comes to proper Italian cuisine. From pizzas to bruschetta, calzones and tiramisu, they've got it covered. The Bufalina pizza is one of our favourites, topped with buffalo mozzarella, cherry tomatoes and olio extra virgin olive oil – this is sure to give you that authentic taste of Italy we all know and love.

Where: Oi Vita, Islington

4. Tierra Peru

A post shared by Tierra Peru (@tierraperu) on Mar 16, 2016 at 1:29am PDT

If you're after something a bit different, Tierra Peru is the place for you. These guys bring authentic Peruvian goodies here to the UK, and we're so glad they did. We love the Cebiche Tradicional here, where fish is marinated in lime-juice, aji limo, red onion and coriander. For a really classic feast, sub on a side dish of either fried sweet plantain, sweet potato or fried cassava fingers. Enjoy your evening Peruvian style.

Where: Tierra Peru, Islington

5. Thai Green Curry, Tootoomoo

A post shared by Tootoomoo (@tootoomooldn) on Jan 10, 2017 at 11:07am PST

Thai cuisine is always a good call on takeaway night, so check out the Thai Green Curry from the guys at Tootoomoo. This classic dish is served up with succulent tiger prawns bursting with those unforgettable traditional Thai flavours. And the good news doesn't stop there, sub out the prawns for a mixed vegetable medley and you've got a real vegetarian treat on your hands. Everyone can get in on the fresh, tangy fun of the Thai green curry here.

Where: Tootoomoo, Islington

6. 8 Inch Mile High Chocolate Salted Caramel Cake, Hummingbird Bakery

A post shared by The Hummingbird Bakery (@hummingbbakery) on Jun 26, 2017 at 2:38am PDT

Craving all things sweet? Then the 8 Inch Mile High Chocolate Salted Caramel Cake from the Hummingbird Bakery is the one for you. Get ready for six layers of rich chocolate sponge cake, filled with sweet salted caramel and topped with dreamy chocolate frosting. And all cakes here are freshly baked and use authentic recipes straight from the USA, so you know they're the real deal.

Where: Hummingbird Bakery, Islington

7. Stir Fried Vegetables, Mildreds

A post shared by Mildreds Restaurants (@mildredsrestaurants) on Jan 17, 2017 at 4:12am PST

Mildreds caters to just about all the foodie cravings out there, whipping up a vast selection of vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free eats. You'll find a great menu full of healthy, internationally inspired food here, just check out the Stir-Fried Vegetables. A mix of tofu, brown jasmine rice and kimchi is met with tenderstem broccoli, baby corn and peppers and sizzled in a sweet soy sauce with peanuts.

Where: Mildreds, Islington

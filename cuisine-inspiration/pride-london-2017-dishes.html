Pride in London dishes on Deliveroo

  • 30 June 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 6 min
pride in london meatliquor bubbledogs burgers rainbow
Share this article

Rainbow flags and glitter at the ready, because we're celebrating all things Pride in London! We're the official catering partner for the event in London this year, so we'll be helping to fuel your parade with amazing eats and these specially designed, one-off dishes that you can find on Deliveroo.

MEATliquor – DEAD HAPPIE™

The burger legends at MEATliquor have pulled out all the stops for this year's festival with the DEAD HAPPIE™. Putting a fabulous spin on the immense DEAD HIPPIE™, this colourful creation consists of a juicy beef patty, sandwiched between a pink bun that's been showered in glitter. It's Pride perfection. Snap one up at the Pride in London parade at Trafalgar Square on Saturday 8th July.  

Available at the Pride in London Parade, at Trafalgar Square, 8th July.

Bubbledogs – Purple Rain

For a hot dog as epic as the Prince song that lends its name to the dish, check out the Purple Rain from Bubbledogs. This delicious dog is topped with southern BBQ sauce, buttermilk coleslaw and copious amounts of purple glitter. And for a Pride-worthy side, you'll have to dunk your fries in the rainbow aioli. Head to Pride in London HQ at Trafalgar Square on Saturday 8th July to pick up one of these puppies.

Available at the Pride in London Parade, at Trafalgar Square, 8th July.

Motu – Rainbow Feast

For Pride on a plate, dig into this Rainbow Feast from Motu. Prawn biryani, cool raita and rainbow pappadums – that's a proper festival of flavours right there. If the spirit of Pride in London has given you all the feels this year, you could spread the love and let your mates have a taste. Then again, some things are just too good to share.

Available from 26th June at Battersea, Canary Wharf, Camberwell.

YO! Sushi – Rainbow Rolls

No one likes hitting a wall in the middle of an all-day party, especially when it's Pride in London. When you need a light bite to perk you up mid-afternoon, check out these sunny Rainbow Rolls from YO! Sushi.

Available in selected restaurants from 26th June: Bond Street, Russell Square, Baker Street, High Street Kensington, St Paul's, Harvey Nichols, Finchley Road, Fulham and Southbank.

Byron – Pride & Joy

Raise a glass to all things Pride in London with Byron. To celebrate this year's festival, these purveyors of proper hamburgers are giving away free cans of Vocation Pride & Joy* – a crisp American pale ale that's going to go down a treat with a Classic Cheese.

*When you spend over £20 on your favourite Byron hamburger and sides, you can get one free can. When you spend over £30 on a festival-worthy feast, you'll get two free cans. Cheers, Byron!

Available from 8th July at Oxford Circus, Beak St, Haymarket, Rathbone Place and Store Street.

Burger King – The Proud Whopper

Get those flags in the air because the Proud Whopper is back! The Burger King classic has donned its rainbow colours for another year to spread a simple message in a delicious way; that we are all the same inside.

Available in selected restaurants (Queensway and Peckham) on 8th and 9th July.

Crust Bros – Hot Love Pizza

This heart-shaped sourdough masterpiece from Crust Bros shows exactly how we all feel about pizza and Pride in London. If you love pizza, love Pride and love salami, fiery n'duja with extra chillies as much as we do, then this is the feast you need after a full-on day out at the parade.

Available from Crust Bros from 28th June.

Benito's Hat – Rainbow Salsa and Tortilla Chips

For a Pride in London fiesta, get a load of Benito's Hat's Rainbow Salsa. This isn't your average chip and dip, this multi-coloured Mexican feast is a six salsa sharing box, with flavours ranging from sweet and spicy pineapple, to tangy cranberry, and fiery salsa brava.

Available at Covent Garden, Farringdon, Bromley, Oxford Circus and Goodge Street from 24th June to 9th July.

Don't miss these limited edition Pride in London dishes on Deliveroo. And pop down to the parade on 8th July to show your support, and sample a DEAD HAPPIETM and a Purple Rain from MEATliquor and Bubbledogs.

Look out for special Pride in London packaging when you order from some of our amazing partner restaurants, including: YO! Sushi, Crust Bros, MEATliquor, Bubbledogs, Motu, Burger King, Byron, Benito's Hat, Mamuska, Cocotte, GBK, BRGR Co., Tommi's Burger Joint, Busaba, Obica and Le Tran Cafe.

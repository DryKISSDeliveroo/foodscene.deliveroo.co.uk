So Long Cheddar! 5 Trendy, Indulgent Cheeses To Elevate Your Burger

  • 30 January 2018
  • Jessy James
  • Read time: 3 min
american vegetarian burgers cheese newcastle london oxford
Share this article

Is a nice thick, juicy burger your go-to weekend treat? We don't blame you! But if you're looking to jazz up your burger toppings and sample different flavours, it can be hard to know which way to turn.

That's why we've done the hard work for you to show you some of the proper naughty burgers being crafted in Deliveroo's American takeaway restaurants up and down the country, featuring new and interesting cheeky cheese toppings.

1. Blue Cheese

A post shared by Fat Hippo (@fathippofood) on Feb 28, 2016 at 2:03am PST

Fat Hippo is one of Newcastle's most popular haunts for those seeking messy, indulgent burgers. These guys certainly don't scrimp on their fillings, which is music to the ears of those wanting a juicy burger as a weekend treat following a busy week at work. We're particularly fond of Stinky Pete – no, it's not the name of one of their staff members! – which features two 4oz patties topped with streaky smoked bacon, spicy chorizo, blue cheese and the signature Fat Hippo sauce.

2. Gorgonzola

A post shared by Brigi Ruszó (@brigi.ruszo) on Nov 5, 2017 at 9:24am PST

If you're looking for freshly made prime British beef burgers in the Oxford area, look no further than Craft Burger. These guys manage to cram shed-loads of flavour in between their sweet brioche buns. We were particularly enamoured with their Oxford Blue burger featuring a slice of rich gorgonzola that melts and oozes wonderfully over the 6oz burger, the caramelised onions and fresh salad. Who knew that blue cheese would work so well with burgers?!

3. Halloumi

The Roti Brothers have gone from strength to strength since starting out in south-east London in a food truck. Their delicious burgers and charcoal rotisserie chicken has taken food to new levels and we're delighted that they are now one of our participating restaurants. Their burgers are the real deal, with even their vegetarian Veggie Bro burger proving popular with meat-eaters alike. The salty addition of halloumi cheese contrasts wonderfully with the sweet beetroot slaw and spicy chimichurri mayo.

4. Mozzarella

A post shared by The Fat Walrus, SE14 (@fatwalruspub) on Oct 11, 2016 at 4:26am PDT

The Fat Walrus gives their customers the choice of having either a beef, veggie, vegan or chicken burger patty as part of their Build-a-Burger menu. For a real taste of America's west coast, the Californian Burger is guaranteed to put a smile on your face. Topped with gooey mozzarella, Mexican-influenced guacamole and sun-blushed tomatoes, it's a festival of flavours in a bun.

5. Swiss Cheese

A post shared by The Pear Tree @ GreenlandPlace (@peartreeatgreenlandplace) on Oct 24, 2017 at 8:37am PDT

Inspired by the diverse culinary trends Down Under, The Pear Tree @ Greenland Place is one of the best places to order a gourmet burger in the Surrey Quays area. For a genuine flavour of America, make sure you get your lips around their Deli burger, featuring crispy buttermilk chicken, melted and indulgent Swiss cheese and topped with a garlic and dill sauerkraut, pickles and a mustard mayo.

Discover the full list of Deliveroo's participating American takeaways, serving juicy burgers, ribs and wings so you can pump some fun back into dinner time tonight!

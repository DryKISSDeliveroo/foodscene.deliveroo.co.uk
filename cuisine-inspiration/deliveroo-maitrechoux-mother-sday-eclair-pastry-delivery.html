d’éclair-ations of love, the perfect Mother’s Day gift

  • 5 March 2018
  • Andy Nolan
  • Read time: 2 min
deliveroo maitre choux mother's day eclair delivery
Share this article

Cards and flowers are a nice gift, but what if you could give Mum something really unique this Mother's Day? Enter pâtisserie extraordinaires Maitre Choux, who have created four delicious éclairs topped with "D'Éclair-ations" of love.

Made with the finest pastry and freshest seasonal ingredients, they come in four fabulous flavours – Salted Butter & Caramel, Persian Pistachio, Spanish Raspberry, and Very Dark Chocolate and are available from Maitre Choux's Kensington and Soho stores.

The "D'Éclair-ations" themselves, 'I'm moving back home', 'I'm your fault' and 'Your card is in the post', are short and sweet – there's only so much you can fit on an éclair. You can order the éclairs exclusively via Deliveroo for £25 from March 5th, available whilst stocks last.

We'll leave you with a word from Jeremie at Maitre Choux, "We aim to create elegant and unique patisseries for every occasion. With the of help of Deliveroo, we are distributing D'Éclair-ations of Love across the capital, giving Londoners every reason to treat their mum this Mother's Day."

Make Mum's day with Maitre Choux's cracking sweet creations, and order on Deliveroo from their Soho or Kensington stores.

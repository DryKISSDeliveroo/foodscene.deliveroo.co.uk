When It Comes To A Mother’s Day Breakfast, Leeds Mums Will Be Winning At Life

  • 8 March 2018
  • Katie Backster
  • Read time: 4 min
eggs pancakes chocolate breakfast vegetarian leeds brunch
Share this article

Sunday, March 11th, 2018 is a date that should be written in the heaviest black ink in every son and daughter's diary. It's the day of the year when we will all show our mums just how much they mean to us with gifts, flowers – and even breakfast or brunch.

That's right. We suggest you start the day as you mean to go on by treating Mum to one of the tastiest breakfasts Leeds has to offer. There are plenty of places to order breakfast takeaway from, so it's going to be easy to pick the perfect one for her. Then all you have to do is deliver it to her in bed and start planning the rest of the day's celebrations!

1. Avocado and Eggs Royale - Le Chalet

Begin the day the perfect way by treating your mum like the queen she really is. For get-up-and-go personalities, may we recommend the Eggs Royale with a side order of avocado from Le Chalet? It's a classic eggs benedict covered with a silky hollandaise sauce but with the extra luxury of smoked salmon. It's even served on their own homemade bread to make it a start to the day that she's sure to really adore.

2. Yorkshire Breakfast - IF Coffee Bar

A post shared by Brad Howe (@bradhazard) on Mar 10, 2018 at 2:48am PST

If mum's a bit more of a traditionalist, then maybe the Yorkshire Breakfast from If Coffee Bar could be the perfect choice. We all love eggs and the first thing they ask at If is how you'd like them done. These will join a plateful of traditional Yorkshire fare including sausage, bacon, mushrooms tomatoes and hash browns, not forgetting the toast, of course. So all that's left for you to do is pop the kettle on for a true Yorkshire brew.

3. Chocolate Pancakes - Crepe Affaire

A post shared by Trinity Leeds- Crepeaffaire (@leedscrepeaffaire) on Feb 4, 2014 at 12:26pm PST

Maybe mum's got a sweet tooth. In that case, here's the perfect solution from Crepe Affaire. They're famous throughout the city for their sublime pancakes and none are finer than their chocolate variety. They only use the very best Belgian chocolate and promise that there's never going to be any sugary syrup drenching out the delicate flavours. Of course, if you feel like giving her an even special treat, we also hear that their Nutella pancakes have quite a lot going for them too.

4. Huevos Rancheros - All Bar One

Just because it's a special day it doesn't mean that it can't be an adventurous one too. So encourage her to take a walk on the wild side with Huevos Rancheros from All Bar One. After all, it's how they'll be celebrating Mother's Day down Mexico way. Two perfectly poached eggs come on top of a grilled tortilla with pico de gallo and black bean salsa plus a special spicy tomato sauce.

5. Eggs Benedict - White's Café

A post shared by T A S T Y T U C K E R (@tastytucker) on Oct 28, 2017 at 9:10am PDT

Of course, mum may prefer things to be plain and simple, prepared to perfection. In which case, we suggest a classic Eggs Benedict from White's Café. It comes with hand-carved ham. For a more power-packed start to the day, how about Eggs Florentine with spinach instead? If it was good enough for Popeye, it's good enough for mum!

Remember, Deliveroo is here to help make Mother's Day for you. Pick one of these eggsellent breakfasts to make mum's day!

Fill The Void With Manchester’s Tastiest Burrito Fillings

  • 15 February 2018
  • Jessy James
  • Read time: 4 min
burrito mexican spicy manchester latin south american takeaway
Share this article

There's no doubt that the best part of digging into any juicy burrito is that initial burst of flavour following the first bite. Healthy, fresh and authentic ingredients all wrapped up in a warm tortilla is guaranteed to put a smile on your face any day of the week! If you're looking to wrap your hands around some of the best-tasting burritos from the heart of Mexico served right here in Manchester, check out our guide to these unique burrito fillings:

1. Tinga De Pollo - Burrito Picante

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Popsicle Productions (@popsicleproductions) on Apr 17, 2017 at 12:31am PDT

Starting off with a classic, the Tinga De Pollo burrito from Burrito Picante sets a fine example for burrito lovers and newcomers alike. Toasty tortilla bread is stuffed to the brim with a combination of succulent braised chicken and all the traditional beloved toppings you crave, including diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sour cream and refried beans. Topped off with a drizzle of chipotle sauce, this burrito has it all. For all you weight watchers, the Tinga De Pollo comes with a naked option as well so you can skip the tortilla and dive right into the incredible fillings without a worry!

2. Beef Barbacoa - Pancho's Burritos

Those looking for a beefier edge to their burritos, look no further than the Beef Barbacoa from Pancho's Burritos. Tender shreds of barbacoa beef drenched in an authentic chipotle sauce are wrapped in your choice of a wide range of tortillas including the classic tortilla, spinach or tomato options. Healthy additions include brown or Mexican herb-infused rice, savoury cheeses and refreshing sour cream provide the tastiest accompaniment to this ultimate beef burrito. An absolute must-try for any barbacoa fanatic, this award-winning restaurant is worth checking out the next time you feel a rumble coming on!

3. Surf 'n' Turf - Chilango

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Chilango (@chilango_uk) on Aug 19, 2016 at 4:16am PDT

Bringing a whole new world of excitement to the average burrito experience by combining delicious ingredients fresh from both land and sea is none other than the Surf 'n' Turf from Chilango. Thick, tender slices of grilled steak set the base of this burrito topped with juicy prawns, black beans, Mexican rice, basil and sour cream all generously layered to create a mountain of flavour. This classic mix of steak and seafood makes the Surf 'n' Turf a burrito you can't refuse. Those looking to add their own twist to this delectable wrap can opt to include extra grilled chicken, steak, prawns or even roasted pork belly to the mix for an even tastier customised meal, all available at Chilango.

4. Barburmeato - Barburrito

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Barburrito (@barburritouk) on Aug 14, 2017 at 3:33am PDT

Presenting a burrito that speaks for itself, the Barburmeato is a legendary burrito proudly served right here at Barburrito. Offering a variety of meaty goodness to choose from, the Barburmeato is guaranteed to satisfy those Latin food cravings. Grilled steak, succulent pork, or tender diced chicken are offered alongside fresh fillings of your choice such as white lime-rice or the classic Mexican brown rice, spicy black or pinto beans, fresh mushrooms, crispy onions, fajita-style peppers and more to create your all-time perfect burrito experience. Every addition of your choice brings forth a new level of zest that you can mix and match for a new uplifting burrito anytime!

If you're looking to dig into some of the best burritos in Manchester, look no further! Deliveroo is here and ready to bring any of these perfectly packed bundles of goodness to your front step.

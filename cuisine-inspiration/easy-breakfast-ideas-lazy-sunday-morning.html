Make breakfast easy like Sunday morning: Breakfast delivery in Leeds

  • 23 October 2017
  • Kieran Andrews
  • Read time: 3 min
breakfast brunch sunday eggs toast pancakes waffles leeds
Share this article

For many of us, Sunday mornings are the only chance in the week we get to take it easy and there's no need to rush off anywhere. But just because you've got time on your hands, it doesn't mean that you want to spend that time cooking breakfast. Why not give yourself the ultimate lazy Sunday by ordering breakfast to be delivered instead?

Whether it's just for you and the kids, or you've got friends coming over for brunch, many of the local restaurants in your area will be able to rustle up a breakfast spread to suit, and bring it to your door at whatever time you're ready to eat.

Getting breakfast delivered also allows you to think outside of the normal breakfasts you might prepare yourself, and to try something new.

1. Graze

There are some healthy options on the Graze breakfast menu, but who really wants Granola or Porridge on a Sunday morning? Our advice is to go for the Graze Breakfast Box. It comes with two rashers of bacon, black pepper sausage, eggs, mushroom and oven roasted tomatoes, with toast on the side. There's a Veggie option too, with grilled halloumi in place of the sausage and bacon.

A post shared by If-Coffee Bar (@ifcoffeebar) on Jan 23, 2017 at 4:28am PST

2. Olive & Rye

At Olive & Rye, there's a big choice of brunch suggestions, including crumpets, granola, waffles and french toast. However, our favourite pick is the Buttermilk Pancakes, which comes with lashings of salted caramel, vanilla cream and Darkwoods espresso. To complement any of the brunch mains, there are a number of sides you can add to your order.

A post shared by @the_boy_loves_food on Oct 4, 2017 at 10:32pm PDT

3. Hepworth's Deli

Another one for those who like something sweet in the morning is the Brioche French Toast from Hepworths's Deli. It's served with fresh strawberries, vanilla and maple yoghurt. Maybe you'll boost your fresh fruit intake by ordering one of their smoothies to go with it - choose between red berry, apple and banana or banana, oats, vanilla and honey.

4. HIVE

The HIVE English Breakfast Bake is a kind of breakfast fusion food. It's a got elements of the traditional full English but it also resembles a pizza. The tasty combination of eggs, pork mince, haricot beans in a house-made tomato sauce along with spinach, onions and parmesan will silence any tummy rumbling and set you up for the day. This would definitely work as an easy-to-hold breakfast in bed idea!

A post shared by Hive_food (@hive_food) on Jul 4, 2017 at 4:21am PDT

5. Jack-Pots

This Leeds restaurant takes the humble jacket potato to new heights, but they also do breakfast.

A post shared by Amber Gold (@_ambergold) on Sep 24, 2015 at 5:23am PDT

Next time you fancy a traditional Sunday morning treat without getting the frying pan out, order the Full English Breakfast from Jack-Pots. Available as a custom-build choice, simply pick four or eight pieces from the breakfast selection. Choices include mushrooms, cumberland sausage, smoked back bacon, hash browns, tomatoes, baked beans, eggs the way you like them, and toast.

There you have it; five great breakfasts that can be brought to your door. Make this Sunday a lazy one and order your breakfast with Deliveroo.

Mother’s Day: a full day of foodie ideas for Mum

  • 24 March 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 4 min
mother's day cupcakes brunch dinner ideas sunday lunch
Share this article

If you want to pull out all the stops for Mother's Day but so far all you've got planned is a last minute dash to the supermarket for bagels and a bunch of flowers, we've listed a full day of meal ideas to make Mum's day a special one.

From breakfast to bubbly, you'll find everything you need to create the mother of all Mother's Day feasts with a little help from these restaurants on Deliveroo.

Breakfast - Core Collective, Kensington

A post shared by Core Collective (@corecollective) on Aug 19, 2016 at 5:16am PDT

Boiling an egg is enough to bring some people out in a panic, so if this sounds like you, put down the saucepan and let Core Collective take care of breakfast for you. Core Collective is the perfect way to start Mother's Day bright and early with some guilt-free goodies. Choose from the likes of dairy-free coconut porridge topped with seasonal compote, or an Instagram-friendly plate of smashed avocado.

Breakfast - Snaps & Rye, Notting Hill

A post shared by SNAPS + RYE (@snapsandrye) on Jan 7, 2017 at 2:32am PST

With pastries, porridge and perfectly scrambled eggs, create a mini banquet this Mother's Day breakfast at Snaps & Rye. Have a good old natter with Mum over Snaps & Rye's buttery Danish pastries, followed by  house-cured salmon with scrambled eggs on rye to set you up for a big day out.

Brunch - The Hyndland Fox, Glasgow

A post shared by The Hyndland Fox (@thehyndlandfox) on Mar 17, 2017 at 4:28am PDT

Not in a rush to kick off Mother's Day too early? Then start your Sunday the lazier way at The Hyndland Fox. Whether Mum fancies brioche French toast piled high with bacon and maple syrup or smashed avocado on Turkish toast, The Hyndland Fox will see you raise the bar for future Mother's Day brunches – just make sure you can top it next year.

Sunday Roast - The Imperial, King's Road

A post shared by The Imperial Arms (@theimperialarms) on Jan 31, 2016 at 5:25am PST

It's not Sunday without a classic roast dinner and Mother's Day should be no exception. But don't stress over what spuds to make or the prospect of spending time in the kitchen with your siblings, The Imperial's selection of lemon and lime roast chicken, five spice roasted pork loin or an extra special côte de bœuf will guarantee a hassle-free (and very fancy) family lunch that will go very nicely with a freshly popped bottle of prosecco.

Lunch - Tandoor Chophouse, Covent Garden

A post shared by Tandoor Chop House (@tandoorchop) on Mar 17, 2017 at 10:22am PDT

Or, break with tradition and swap the Sunday roast for a feast from Tandoor Chophouse. Combining North Indian flavours with prime cuts of meat, fish and paneer, Tandoor Chophouse delivers beautifully spiced dishes and knockout sides of Gunpowder Fries and bone marrow naan that are perfect for sharing.

Lunch - Rosa's Thai Café, locations across London

A post shared by Rosa's Thai Cafe (@rosasthaicafe) on Jan 26, 2017 at 7:56am PST

For a light and fragrant lunch, sample some of the vibrant salads from Rosa's Thai Café. Topped with papaya, spicy beef or chicken, these Thai dishes with a modern twist are the perfect alternative to a heavier Sunday roast if an active day around the shops is on the cards.

Afternoon Tea - Macarons & More, Norwich

A post shared by Macarons & More (@macarons_and_more) on Mar 20, 2017 at 10:50am PDT

For a tea-riffic afternoon tea, macarons are a must. Macarons & More's selection boxes are almost too cute to eat (we said almost!), and these chic French favourites will add a little je ne sais quoi to your Mother's Day feast.

Idea for the Evening - Tapas Brindisa, Shoreditch

A post shared by Brindisa (@brindisaspanishfoods) on Mar 19, 2017 at 4:18am PDT

For an evening meal full of flavour, Tapas Brindisa's menu of classic tapas dishes and house specials are ideal for a fuss-free Mother's Day. Inspired by the Mediterranean coast, their selection of tapas staples, including patatas bravas and cured meats and cheese, can be perfectly paired with sherry or cava – because what's Mother's Day without a bit of bubbly.

Idea for the Evening - Ceviche, Soho, Old Street and Shoreditch

A post shared by Ceviche (@cevicheuk) on Mar 18, 2017 at 12:02pm PDT

Ceviche serves up some of the prettiest plates of food in town. Their light and bright Peruvian dishes are packed full of exciting flavours and plenty of flair, with a wide range of ceviche (of course) and cooked specialities on the menu.

Idea for the Evening - Sticks 'n' Sushi, Cambridge, Greenwich, Canary Wharf, Wimbledon

A post shared by S T I C K S | N | S U S H I (@sticksnsushi) on Feb 28, 2017 at 12:22am PST

Taking charge of the Mother's Day menu is no easy task, but with Sticks 'n' Sushi you'll tick all the boxes for the most champion chopstick-user in the family to nigiri newbies.

Feed the family with Deliveroo

  • 13 September 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 7 min
kids' meals kids' menu family meals family bundles
Share this article

You've got your favourite dishes on Deliveroo – the ones you get for an office lunch or for those quiet nights to yourself when you don't feel like cooking. But did you know we can help you keep the kids happy at meal times too? Check out all the tasty meal ideas for the little ones on Deliveroo.

Carluccio's Bambini menu – Nationwide

If you've been working late but still want to eat with the kids before they go to bed, you could give the cooking a miss for the night and order Deliveroo on your way home. Why not get something from Carluccio's Bambini menu for them and a main dish for you? You could pick from a mini Macaroni Cheese to a Chicken Milanese.

Stonebaked Pizza Co's Kids' Pizzas – Leicester

A post shared by Stonebaked Pizza Co (@stonebakedpizzaco) on Oct 29, 2015 at 4:35am PDT

If you're throwing a kid's party, the catering is always the tricky part. Getting something that keeps everyone happy and saves you a mountain of work in the kitchen is the dream, right? So how about getting some Kids' Pizzas from Stonebaked Pizza Co delivered? These build-your-own pizzas are perfect for little people, because they're just the right size and they can choose their own sauce and toppings.

The Chilli Pickle Canteen Kids – Brighton

Working away through the week can be tough, and when you get back the first thing you want to do is spend time with the kids. So don't worry about getting stuck in the kitchen, we'll bring The Chilli Pickle Canteen to you. You and your other half can get your favourite curries to share, and the little ones can get their own Kids Box. They get a choice of a lightly spiced curry, salad, chutney, naan and rice, plus there's even a bag of sweet treats for afters.

Canterbury Tails' Kids' Menu – Canterbury

Fish and chips is a classic, right? There's nothing like that smell of salt and vinegar when you all dig into the bag to pick out your order. But a grown-up portion of cod can feel like a whale for the little ones. So check out Canterbury Tails' Kids Menu for kiddy-sized Cod Meals, Scampi or Cod Nuggets. That way, they get just the right portion and, more importantly, you won't have to divide your favourite from the chippy with one of the kids.

The Loving Hut Kids' Menu – Brighton

If you're looking for a sneaky way to get more veggies in the kids' diets, The Loving Hut is the way to go. This vegan café puts a plant-based spin on kiddie classics like nuggets and hot dogs with their Golden Nuggets, Fish Fingers and a Not-Dog with Chips.

Byron's Mini Byron menu – Nationwide

A post shared by Byron 🍔 (@byronhamburgers) on Oct 24, 2016 at 8:59am PDT

Remember when you were a kid, wasn't it just the best day ever when Mum or Dad said you were having burgers for dinner? Well the burger game has been raised since we were young, this generation's got Mini Byron. They can pick a little person version of Byron's proper hamburgers, with the Mini Classic, Mini Chicken or Mini Bean Patty to choose from. There's even a Mini Brownie and a Mini Milkshake – if they've been good and finished all their mains of course! These are little portions that are bound to get a big smile.

Roosters Piri Piri Kids' Menu – Watford

Weekends are for chilling. So if you've got a houseful already and don't really fancy prepping lunch for everyone, we can sort it out. Something like Roosters Piri Piri Kids Menu can take care of the little ones. There's some well balanced choices for them, like a Chicken Wrap – which comes either plain, or flavoured with lemon and herb or a Lebanese spice blend – and a Sweet Potato Falafel Wrap for any non-meat-eaters in your clan. That's lunch sorted then.

Pinche Pinche's Children's Meal – Leeds

Pinche Pinche does delicious Mexican food for mini people. The Cheese Quesadilla or the Chicken Quesadilla are great for an easy midweek meal. They're full of vibrant flavours, squished between two toasty tortillas, that are perfect for little hands to pick up and eat, or they can cut them up easily into little bite-sized pieces. Plus, they come with a side salad (so you can get some more green good stuff onto their plates) and a portion of fries that you could pinche pinche when they're not looking!

Meatcure's Family Feast bundle – Leicester

Hit a midweek slump? There's no better way to get over hump day than with a round of burgers for you and the family. So if you're on the train home from work, and can't stop thinking about a Royale with Cheese, just hit up the Family Feast from Meatcure. You and your better half can pick your favourite burgers, plus fries, and then the kids can get two Mini Royales with fries – all that in one beast of a bundle.

Bubble Boba's Family Meal Deal – Coventry

A hot day and some ice cold milkshakes makes some little people very happy. But there's no reason why you should miss out. So when you're all due a bit of a chill, check out the Family Meal Deal from Bubble Boba's. Pick three shakes from the Milkshakes Favourites menu and the last one's free. So what's it going to be; Cookies & Cream all round, or are you going to mix it up with a Bubblegum one, maybe a Cherry Bakewell and a Salted Caramel Banana?

Bluegrass BBQ Kids – High Wycombe

Everyone loves a family BBQ. But if burnt bangers are more your forte, leave it to the pitmasters at Bluegrass BBQ. Their Kids' Meals are pint-sized BBQ perfection, with small person versions of everything from pulled Boston butt Pork to BBQ Beans n Sausages. Plus, if they're into sweet and salty combos just as much as you are, there's a little portion of sausages, bacon and maple syrup piled on top of fluffy pancakes that'll be perfect for your little short stacks.

Giraffe Kids' Menu – Nationwide

Do you ever get into a routine of mealtimes through the week, and kind of feel like your repertoire is on a bit of a rota? Well if you want to switch things up, but you've run out of ideas, you need to give Giraffe's Kids' Menu a go. There's loads of bright and healthy dishes that you can order in. You've got a choice of summery Sunshine Salads and Pasta al Pomodoro if your little ones don't like meat, right up to Katsu Chicken on fluffy steamed rice and Grilled Chicken Breast with yummy Sweet Potato Fries on the side.

Keep checking in for extra kids' menus from Giraffe, there's more coming in September, just in time for those busy weeks back at school.

G'rillers' Kids' Menu – High Wycombe

A post shared by G'rillers (@grillershw) on Dec 7, 2016 at 12:41am PST

After a long week and a long commute, sometimes the last thing you want to do is cook. So when you and the kids are after something fresh and fun for dinner, try the Kids' Menu from G'rillers. Little ones love any excuse to get a bit messy with their food don't they, so a Sticky Pops Mountain with chips and sweetcorn is great food for little fingers.

Whether you're planning a kids party, or need some midweek meal inspiration, we've got your back. Check out all the tasty kids' meals on Deliveroo that'll keep you and the little ones happy.

Mother’s Day: Sweet Treat Ideas for Mum

  • 24 March 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 3 min
mother's day cupcakes brownies gluten-free vegan
Share this article

We don't mean to question your capabilities as a pastry chef, but sugar work and wizardry with a whisk should sometimes be left to the professionals. That's why we've compiled a list of the best sweet treats on Deliveroo that'll earn you some serious brownie points this Mother's Day.

Orée, Knightsbridge

A post shared by Orée (@oreeboulangerie) on Sep 22, 2016 at 6:52am PDT

Dainty delicacies are an absolute must if you're looking to create a winning afternoon tea for Mother's Day.  Inspired by rural French boulangeries and patisseries, Orée will add a chic twist to your Mother's Day menu with raspberry macarons, classic chocolate eclairs and a selection of traditional tartlettes.

Hummingbird Bakery, Soho

A post shared by The Hummingbird Bakery (@hummingbbakery) on Jan 11, 2017 at 9:06am PST

Get the kettle on and brew up a winning afternoon tea with Hummingbird Bakery's colourful collection of cakes, tray bakes and whoopie pies. A fan favourite at this all-American style bakery is their delicious red velvet, so why not go all out with a full 8-inch cake that's perfect for a Mother's Day tea party.

Mimi's Bakehouse, Leith

A post shared by Mimi's Bakehouse (@mimisbakehouse) on Oct 17, 2016 at 2:59am PDT

Connoisseur of the cupcake Mimi's Bakehouse has been awarded the title of Craft Baker of the Year for a second year running. Available in batches of six or 12, their chocolate-based cupcake selection is a chocoholic's dream that's bound to be winner with Mum for an afternoon treat.

Glutopia, Kingston-upon-Thames

A post shared by Glutopia Bakery (@glut0pia) on Dec 20, 2016 at 4:46am PST

For gooey brownies without the gluten, Glutopia is the place to go. Glutopia serves up boxes of their chewy, chocolately goodness in mixed batches of six or 12. If Mum already has a firm favourite, then why not go for a box of Nutella-topped treats or their salted caramel brownies.

Dominique Ansel - Belgravia

A post shared by Dominique Ansel Bakery London (@dominiqueansellondon) on Mar 21, 2017 at 2:37am PDT

When one treat isn't enough Dominique Ansel has got a full hamper of goodies to keep Mum sweet on Mother's Day. With delicate macarons, almond brittle, popcorn and mini meringues, this hamper will safely secure your favourite child status.

The Pudding Stop, St Albans

A post shared by The Pudding Stop (@thepuddingstop) on Feb 16, 2017 at 1:37am PST

When it's owned by a former Great British Bake Off contestant - Johnny Shepherd - you know it's going to be good. That's why The Pudding Stop is the perfect way to spoil Mum this Mother's Day. The Pudding Stop serves up all your favourite cosy classics, like sticky toffee pudding and blackberry and apple crumble, which is ideal if the weather takes a typically British turn for the worse.

Pearl & Groove, Portobello Road

A post shared by Pearl And Groove 💋 (@pearlandgroove) on Jan 29, 2017 at 1:02pm PST

Pearl & Groove's goodies might be gluten-free but they're certainly full of flavour. These tasty treats swap regular flour for ground almonds so everyone can enjoy a slice of dulche de leche and sea salt or tuck in to a banoffee pie. Or, for a sweet treat that's truly saintly, they even offer gluten-free, dairy-free and sugar-free cakes.

Lola's Cupcakes, Covent Garden

A post shared by Lola's Cupcakes (@lolas_cupcakes) on Jul 26, 2016 at 3:04am PDT

Lola's Cupcakes are not just your go-to guys for cute cupcakes, their selection of brownies, including red velvet, salted caramel and peanut butter, are the perfect pairing for a good cuppa and a catch up with Mum.

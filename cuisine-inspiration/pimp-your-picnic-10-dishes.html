10 dishes to pimp your picnic

  • 21 June 2016
  • Becky Hughes
  • Read time: 5 min
picnic
Share this article

Leave the picnic basket at home - we've got 10 al-fresco friendly picks here to put any potato salad or scotch egg to shame. And the best thing? You can order them right to your picnic patch.

1. Jamie's Superfood Salad, Jamie Oliver's Super Food Lunch

A picnic pot prepared by Jamie Oliver's chefs? Yes, please! Packed with goodies like chargrilled avocado, roasted beets, sprouting broccoli and pomegranate seeds, this crunchy salad comes with a generous helping of harissa dressing.

2. Chicken strips, Mother Clucker

Swap cocktail sausages for these fried chicken strips from Mother Clucker. Twice tea-brined and coated in buttermilk, these strips are seriously crunchy on the outside and super soft in the middle - with a little pot of hot sauce and lime mayo for that all important dunking.

ORDER MOTHER CLUCKER

3. Charcuterie, Chez Elles

No one does picnics better than the French, and Chez Elles' charcuterie is guaranteed to amp up your al-fresco feast. Served with crusty fresh bread, duck rillette and ham hock terrine - just watch out for envious looks from other picnic goers.

ORDER CHEZ ELLES

4. Beef summer rolls, Whaam Banh Mi

We dare you to try to find a more fresh and fragrant summer snack than these Vietnamese rolls. Whaam Banh Mi fill theirs with tender roast beef, homemade pickles and fresh herbs - make sure you don't let even a scrap of these tasty morsels escape onto the picnic blanket.

ORDER WHAAM BANH MI

5. Lamb shoulder kebab, Chifafa

Don't be fooled - Chifafa's lamb shoulder kebab isn't the kind you'd only ever devour on the way home after a tipple or two. With no doners in sight, their lamb is marinated in 16 herbs and spices, then slow-cooked and served in souvlaki bread with feta, green mango pickle, red cabbage slaw and mild chilli sauce.

ORDER CHIFAFA

6. Watermelon juice, Roots Juicery

What with the sun and all that eating, picnics can be thirsty work. Luckily Roots Juicery has a refreshing selection of juices to keep you hydrated and happy. We love the summery Seasonal Juice with watermelon, cucumber, strawberries and mint.

ORDER ROOTS JUICERY

7. Cheeseburger, Bleecker Street

A favourite at food festivals, this cheeseburger from Bleecker Street will bring a true taste of New York to your al-fresco affair. Cooked medium rare as standard and oozing with American cheese, it's juicy and mad messy - best eat it quick!

ORDER BLEECKER STREET

8. Moroccan meatballs hot box, Leon

The lunch legends at Leon know how to cater for a picnic or two. Just try this flavour-packed hot box and you'll know what we mean - grilled meatballs cooked down in a spicy-ish tomato sauce, served with rice, slaw, freshly chopped mint and garlic aioli.

ORDER LEON

9. Oak smoked salmon sandwich, Souli

Ever thought about adding candied orange zest or grated eggs to your sandwich? The chefs at Souli have. Voted one of the Top 5 Sandwiches in London by The Evening Standard, Souli really does take its smoked salmon sarnie to the next level.  

ORDER SOULI

10. Peter Andre Yum Yum, Dum Dum Donutterie

Don't be put off by the name - the only mysterious thing about this baked croissant doughnut is how it tastes so good. Filled with Dulce de Leche creme, drizzled in chocolate and glazed while still warm, it's the ultimate sticky-fingered picnic treat.  

ORDER DUM DUM DONUTTERIE

The best breakfasts for a long lie in

  • 27 October 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 6 min
breakfast brunch pancakes bacon
Share this article

Make your Sunday morning even easier, with these breakfasts and brunches on Deliveroo that are perfect for a long lie in when the clocks go back on 29th October.

Halloumi & Shrooms, Federal Café & Bar – Manchester

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Federal Cafe & Bar (@federalcafebar) on Oct 17, 2017 at 12:05am PDT

No one does laid-back living quite like Australia and New Zealand. So take it easy on Sunday, Antipodean style, with Halloumi & Shrooms from Federal Café & Bar. This Manchester-based restaurant channels Australia and New Zealand's café culture, with all day brunches and breakfasts until 12pm. So don't rush up, we'll take care of breakfast.

Welsh Rarebit Croissant, Dominique Ansel Bakery – London

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Dominique Ansel Bakery London (@dominiqueansellondon) on May 19, 2017 at 1:48am PDT

Dominique Ansel, the bakery that gifted the world with the Cronut, is working its magic on the croissant once again. Everyone's favourite French breakfast pastry has been given a Welsh twist this time, with a Welsh Rarebit Croissant, made using Guinness Worcestershire Cheddar béchamel, whole grain mustard and fontina cheese. We'll happily get out of bed for that much cheese and pastry!

The Breakfast Burger, Haché – London

Burgers for breakfast? Now we're talking. Haché's Breakfast Burger is quite possibly the dream way to start your Sunday when you're feeling a little fragile and only a steak burger, topped with smoked bacon, sweet potato rosti, fried Clarence Court eggs and coffee ketchup will make you feel human again.

Moose Banana & Pecan Pancake Stack, Moose Breakfast and Brunch – Liverpool

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Moose Coffee (@moosecoffee) on Jul 7, 2017 at 4:11am PDT

For a sweeter start to your day, you've got to give Moose Breakfast and Brunch's Moose Banana & Pecan Pancake Stack a try. A breakfast this good should be enjoyed all day, so take your time on Sunday morning then dive into a tower of fluffy pancakes covered in chocolate hazelnut spread, maple pecans and fresh bananas.

Organic Granola, Compote & Yoghurt, Daylesford Organic – London

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by daylesfordfarm (@daylesfordfarm) on Jul 9, 2017 at 11:05pm PDT

Do you ever have those wintery mornings when you crave a bit of sunshine? If you're not quite ready to don your dressing gown and curl up with something cosy, then go for a breakfast pot from Daylesford Organic, layered with silky yoghurt, berry compote and crunchy coconut granola, instead.

Raspberry and Almond Chia Pudding, The Detox Kitchen – London

If you're less of a duvet dweller and more of a get-up-and-go to the gym kind of person, then you can still get something light and delicious delivered. Why not try a Raspberry and Almond Chia Pudding from The Detox Kitchen to kickstart your Sunday? It's gluten free too, so if you're eating clean, this will be your new go-to weekend breakfast.

Avocado, Tomato, Bacon and Scrambled Egg, ZouBisou – Royal Leamington Spa

Even after that extra hour's sleep, getting up on a Sunday's never easy, unless you can smell cooked bacon. So if you need an extra incentive to rise and shine, get creamy scrambled eggs, topped with crispy bacon, avocado and tomato from ZouBisou to get you out of bed.

Full English Breakfast, The Market Café – Worcester

The full English is an icon of the breakfast table. So when time's on your side, you can afford to take it easy with the works from The Market Café. Bacon, sausages, egg, mushrooms, black pudding, beans, tomatoes and a hash brown (be honest, that's what we all get a full English for), plus a free tea or Americano, and that's Sunday sorted.

Bakchich Vegetarian Breakfast, Bakchich – Leeds

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Bakchich Lebanese Street Food (@bakchich_) on Mar 16, 2016 at 1:23am PDT

Veggies shouldn't miss out on a mammoth breakfast. So if you'd rather swap sausages and beans for lightly spiced sumac eggs and vibrant Lebanese flavours, get your big breakfast fix from Bakchich. Their Vegetarian Breakfast comes with halloumi and a side of Arabic bread too, so even meat-eaters will want to mix it up a bit with a breakfast like this.

Breakfast in Bed for Two, Le Pain Quotidien – London

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Le Pain Quotidien UK (@lepainquotidienuk) on Apr 23, 2016 at 11:37am PDT

A solo lazy Sunday's great, but it's even better when you're chilling as a team. So if you want to treat your better half to breakfast, but don't want the hassle of juggling pans and smashing avocados on your morning off, get the Breakfast in Bed for Two from Le Pain Quotidien.

Custard Dipped Brioche with Maple Bacon, Pinkmans – Bristol

Custard dipped brioche with maple bacon? Yep, the sweet and salty breakfast of your dreams does exist, and it's made fresh on site at Pinkmans. This Bristol bakery is a firm favourite in the area, and after one bite of this breakfast brioche, it'll be one of yours too.

JJ's Massive Rasher Sandwich, Pot Bellied Pig – Dublin

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Pot Bellied Pig (@potbelliedpigd6) on Jun 25, 2017 at 2:33am PDT

There are bacon sandwiches and then there's a Pot Bellied Pig bacon sandwich. JJ's Massive Rasher Sandwich stacks five O'Neill's rashers in a soft, cushiony bap from Arun Bakery for the ultimate Sunday recovery kit after a big Saturday night.

Jaffa-Tel Aviv Style, Strut & Cluck – London

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by DelaminaKitchen (@delaminakitchen) on Sep 16, 2017 at 1:33am PDT

If a Sunday morning fry-up isn't your thing, you have to try Strut & Cluck's Jaffa-Tel Aviv Style Brunch. This platter's bursting with sunny Mediterranean colours and flavours. You can pick your eggs – either scrambled, fried or poached – then dig into sides of labneh, feta with honey, zhoug and whipped butter with a pillowy-soft pita.

HIVE Breakfast Bake, HIVE – Leeds

When you need something wholesome and full of good stuff to get you through a sore head on a Sunday, the HIVE Breakfast Bake will put you right. It's a tasty protein-packed bake of eggs, extra egg whites, pork, haricot beans in a homemade tomato sauce with spinach.

For more tasty breakfast and brunch ideas, check out what's on Deliveroo near you.

Deliveroo’s most popular dishes: 2017

  • 20 May 2017
  • Editor
  • Read time: 4 min
popular dishes burgers pizza pad thai
Share this article

Staying in is the new going out – we Brits just love eating restaurant dishes from the comfort of our own homes. After all, who wouldn't want their favourite food delivered straight to their door?

And that trend shows no signs of slowing down in 2017. We've rounded up the most-ordered meals from across the country, with American, Italian and Thai cuisines taking the podium positions.

American runner-up, Dead Hippie Burger – MEATliquor

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by MEATliquor Bars & Diners (@meatgram) on Mar 27, 2017 at 4:20am PDT

There's no surprise that we love a good burger. From the humble fast-food menu snack to gourmet mega-burgers, you can't throw a stone without hitting a burger joint. MEATliquor's Dead Hippie Burger likes to keep things simple. Two mustard-fried patties are packed with lettuce, cheese, pickles, minced white onions and their signature Dead Hippie sauce into a fresh bun. It's London's favourite order, and the country's second most-ordered American dish.

Where: MEATliquor, various locations

American winner, Byron Burger – Byron

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by B Y R O N (@byronhamburgers) on Mar 14, 2016 at 7:36am PDT

Despite all the wonderful burger combinations out there, we seem to like the old favourites – and that's where Byron comes in. Their Byron Burger is simplicity in a bun – a traditional 6oz patty cooked medium, topped off with dry-cured bacon, mature cheddar and fresh salad, on a squishy homemade bun. Why mess with a classic?

Where: Byron, various locations

Italian runner-up, Margherita – VIP Very Italian Pizza

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by #VeryItalianPizza (@v.i.p.veryitalianpizza) on Nov 15, 2014 at 10:43am PST

The traditional margherita is always there for us. Though we love to venture out into the world of gourmet pizza toppings, there's something so comforting about a classic. And at VIP, they do it the proper Italian way – the family have been making pizzas since 1845. It's served up straight from the oven, with melted Fior di Latte mozzarella, tomato, fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.

Where: VIP Very Italian Pizza, Brighton

Italian winner, Penne Giardiniera – Carluccio's

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Kevin Taylor (@paulo_ucello) on Jan 13, 2017 at 10:20am PST

Carluccio's has their pasta down to a fine art. Their Penne Giardinera is a delight for veggies and carnivores alike – a bowlful of giant Pugliese penne topped with shredded courgette, tiny flecks of chilli and liberally graced with fried balls of spinach and cheese. Not only is it delicious, it's also virtuous, with 50p from every order donated to Action Against Hunger. How could anyone say no?

Where: Carluccio's, various locations

Thai runner-up, anything from Banana Tree

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by BananaTree (@bananatreeuk) on Mar 28, 2017 at 6:31am PDT

As a country, we've taken Thai food to our hearts – it's bright, light, and zingy. Banana Tree's menu is so good that everything counts as a runner-up. For us though, it's the Steamed Chicken Dumplings in Fresh Herb Sauce every time. We also love the Tamarind Spicy Aubergine for a Thai vegetarian alternative, with peppers, carrots, spicy bean paste, garlic, ginger and tamarind.

Where: Banana Tree, various locations

Thai winner, Classic Pad Thai – Busaba Eathai

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Busaba Bangkok Thai (@busababangkokthai) on Nov 1, 2016 at 11:05am PDT

There can only be one winner – and it's Busaba Eathai's Classic Pad Thai by a nose. It's a nest of noodles stir-fried with juicy king prawns, egg and succulent tofu. Topped with beansprouts, crushed peanuts for crunch, and dried shrimp for a whack of umami, this top dish is a thrilling mix of textures and flavours. It's no wonder everyone's ordering it.

Where: Busaba Eathai, various locations

Feeling peckish? Go with the crowd and order one of Deliveroo's best-loved dishes, or stray away from the pack and go for something completely different, and we'll bring it straight to your door.

Payday splurge: 12 ways to really push the boat out

  • 31 January 2017
  • Becky Hughes
  • Read time: 4 min
deliveroo trends payday seafood steak drinks
Share this article

You wouldn't usually spend anything close to £1,000 on a takeaway. But when you're ordering a platter of oysters over a padana pizza, and a bottle of vintage wine rather than a bottle of beer, you can expect to fork out a little more.

We're revealing the most expensive items that you can order on Deliveroo in 12 different countries around the world – and the priciest will cost you £950 (hint: it's in London).

So from least expensive to most expensive, here are the top-dollar dinners and drinks you'll find in countries with the Deliveroo app, converted into pounds:

12. Madrid, Spain - Champagne and Jamón Sandwich, £41

A post shared by B de J (@b_de_j) on Feb 20, 2016 at 2:01am PST

Bocadillo de Jamón y Champán is Madrid's answer to London's Bubbledogs, pairing their Champagne with 'jamón' ham sandwiches – all for £41.

11. Milan, Italy - Ravioli with Foie Gras and White Truffle, £68

A post shared by Gourmet 🍒World (@gourmet.world) on Nov 5, 2016 at 6:47am PDT

Foie gras and white truffle – possibly the most luxurious pasta ingredients in the world. This dish from Tartufi & Friends will set you back £68.

10. Dubai, UAE - Fillet Steak, Gaucho, £90

A post shared by Gaucho Dubai (@gauchodubai) on Nov 11, 2016 at 10:03pm PST

Gaucho has an international for steak, so perhaps it's not all that surprising that their 400g fillet steak costs close to £100.

9. Dublin, Ireland - Seafood Tower, Klaw, £102

A post shared by Chenshu Yuan (@svenyuan) on Jun 22, 2016 at 4:43am PDT

With a whole lobster, a whole crab, six Dublin Bay langostines, eight prawns, eight unshucked oysters, chowder and extra crab claws, you'll dish out £102 for Klaw's S.F.A. Tower.

8. Frankfurt, Germany - Filet Mignon, £107

A post shared by Ranch and Sea Steakhouse (@ranchandsea) on Jul 30, 2016 at 12:08am PDT

Ranch & Sea Steakhouse's regular 300g Filet Mignon costs £32. But their 500g Dry Aged Barrel Cut Fillet Mignon costs more than three times that, at £107.

7. Brussels, Belgium - Shellfish platter, £111

A post shared by Nananananananana (@nananosaurus) on Jun 23, 2016 at 3:53am PDT

L'Ecailler du Belga Queen's seafood platter comes with lobster, crab, prawns, langoustines, shrimp and whelk and a £111 price tag.

6. Den Haag, Netherlands - Boxed Sushi Selection, £127

A post shared by (Carina) (maybe) (@cogcogcog17) on Apr 28, 2016 at 1:26pm PDT

Designed for parties, Urban Sushi's Gala Box comes with 100 sushi and 25 pieces of sashimi, including raw salmon, raw tuna, smoked salmon, unagi eel and shrimp Ebi - all for £127.

5. Singapore - Barbecue Platter, £194

A post shared by Meatsmith SG (@meatsmith_sg) on Jan 11, 2016 at 1:53am PST

Fork out just shy of £200 to enjoy Meatsmith's Premium BBQ Platter, with Mishima Ultra wagyu rib eye, beef rib, smoked chicken and pork ribs. Bring on the meat sweats.  

4. Melbourne, Australia - Deluxe Seafood Platter, £207

A post shared by Richmond Oysters (@richmondoysters) on Dec 21, 2016 at 1:16am PST

Richmond Oysters' dreamy seafood platter includes oysters, sashimi, prawns, Moreton Bay bugs, Herve Bay scallops, mussels, calamari, crab and a whole lobster. And the little lot will cost you £207.

3, Hong Kong - Wagyu Tomahawk steak and bone marrow, £230

A post shared by Lily & Bloom (@lilyandbloom) on Oct 26, 2016 at 12:47am PDT

Lily & Bloom's £230 dish really pushes the boat out – Wagyu Tomahawk steak served with roasted bone marrow and a parsley-shallot salad.

2. Paris, France – Beluga caviar and pearls, £352

A post shared by Caviar de Neuvic Life (@caviardeneuvic_life) on Dec 6, 2016 at 6:50am PST

Designed for parties, Urban Sushi's Gala Box comes with 100 sushi and 25 pieces of sashimi, including raw salmon, raw tuna, smoked salmon, unagi eel and shrimp Ebi - all for £127.

1. London, UK – A bottle of 1964 Château Mouton Rothschild, £950

A post shared by Jean Gerard (@jeangerardf1) on Dec 11, 2016 at 7:20pm PST

It's strictly champagne, caviar and fine wines on L'Etranger's Deliveroo menu. And their very finest wine, the 1964 Château Mouton Rothschild, will cost you close to £1,000.

Yes, you'll find caviar and champagne on Deliveroo, but you don't need to spend loads to really push the boat out. If you're looking to impress a date or celebrate a birthday, browse our 'cream of the crop' restaurants.

And don't forget that if you are ordering alcohol, we may ask for ID on delivery. Always remember to drink responsibly. Visit drinkaware.co.uk for more information.

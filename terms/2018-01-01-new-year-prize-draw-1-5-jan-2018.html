2018-01-01 New Year’s Prize Draw, 1-5 Jan 2018

  • 21 December 2017
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
Share this article
  1. Please read these terms and conditions (T&Cs) carefully. These T&Cs will apply to all entrants to the promotion.  If any entrant does not agree with them, they should not participate. A hard copy of the T&Cs can be printed or a copy requested by post, by sending a stamped self-addressed envelope to the address at the end of these T&Cs.
  2. THE PRIZES:  The Promoter (defined below) is offering 5 chances to win a prize of of £1,000 (the Prize). One of the £1,000 prizes will available to eligible entrants each day between 1 and 5 January.      
  3. HOW TO ENTER: Entry into the daily Prize draw is automatic for all Deliveroo customers who:
  1. meet the eligibility criteria set out in item 6 of these T&Cs; and
  2. order from Deliveroo at any time during that day.
  1. HOW TO WIN: Deliveroo will select a Prize winner randomly from all orders placed on the day in which the prize relates by midday the following day.  
  2. CLAIMING THE PRIZE: The Prize winner will be notified by email.  Identification of the account holder, and a valid bank account will be required before the money is transferred. If the prize has not been claimed within 7 days, it will be redrawn.    
  3. ELIGIBILITY: Promotion is open to all UK customers aged 18 or over who are able to order through their Deliveroo account, excluding employees of the Promoter, and any other person whom, in the Promoter's reasonable opinion, should be excluded due to their involvement or connection with this promotion.
  4. The Promoter reserves the right to cancel or amend this Promotion due to events or circumstances arising beyond its control and, where necessary, to undertake all such action as is reasonable to protect itself against fraudulent or invalid redemptions including, without limitation, to require further verification as to the identity, age, and other relevant details of a customer.
  5. Subject to item 9 of these terms, the Promoter will only use the personal details supplied in customer's accounts, and in connection with this promotion for the administration and delivery of an order, and the contact and verification of the prize winner and for no other purpose without first getting consent.
  6. By accepting any prize, the winner may be required to acknowledge that their name and image may be used for printing, broadcast, publicity and promotional purposes, including but not limited to the Promoter's websites and social media accounts without compensation. The winner may also be asked to participate in publicity arising from the competition.  A winner will, if required by the Promoter, complete any documentation necessary to give effect to this assignment of right, title and interest.
  7. The Promoter may modify, suspend or terminate the promotion (e.g. extend or change the period during which it runs) or make any other changes to the promotion or these T&C.  Where reasonably possible, such changes will be notified in advance by posting amended T&Cs on this page and/or via the Promoters' social pages.
  8. Normal registration and Deliveroo delivery terms and conditions will apply to all orders – see https://deliveroo.co.uk/legal for more information.

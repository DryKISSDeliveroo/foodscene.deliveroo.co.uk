DELIVEROO PROMOTION RULES

These are the terms and conditions ("Rules") that apply to the Signed Lioness Home Shirt Promotion ("Promotion"). By entering the Promotion, each entrant agrees to these Rules and confirms they have read and understood the Deliveroo Privacy Notice at https://deliveroo.co.uk/privacy.

  1. Entry Period: Entry opens at 00:01 (UK time) on 07/05/2020 and closes at 23:59 (UK time) on 10/05/2020 (the "Entry Period").
  1. Eligibility: 
  1. To participate in the Promotion, entrants must be a legal resident of England, Scotland and Wales and at least 18 years old at the time of entry. Residents or Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland are excluded.
  2. Internet access and a Deliveroo account are required to enter the Promotion.
  3. Purchase is necessary to enter.
  4. Deliveroo reserves the right at any time to require proof of eligibility in such form as Deliveroo reasonably considers necessary.

Not eligible: The following persons are not eligible to enter the Promotion: (i) any employee or other individual engaged by Deliveroo or any of its associated companies; (ii) members of the immediate family and/or household (whether related or not) of any of those individuals described in limb (i) above; and (iii) anyone else professionally involved with the operation or administration of the Promotion.

  1. How to enter: To enter the Promotion, entrants must complete the following steps during the Entry Period:
  1. Log into the entrant's Deliveroo account on www.deliveroo.co.uk or the Deliveroo app; and
  2. enter the following promotional code ENGSHIRT (the "Promotion Code") before placing a Qualifying Order.

A "Qualifying Order" means a paid-for Deliveroo order of a minimum value of £10.00 (excluding tips, delivery charges and other service-related charges) during the Entry Period. Each Qualifying Order made with a Promotion Code gives an entrant one entry into the prize draw.

  1. Prize: There is one prize to be won in the Promotion, consisting of a signed and framed England Lionesses home jersey (the "Prize").
  1. Prize conditions: The following conditions apply to the Prize:
  1. The Prize winner must be aged 18 or over.
  1. Only one Prize may be won per person.
  1. The Prize is non-transferable, non-refundable, non-exchangeable and no cash alternative is available. However, if Deliveroo considers it appropriate, or if it becomes necessary for reasons beyond Deliveroo's control, the Prize may be varied or swapped for a suitable alternative in Deliveroo's discretion.
  1. Winner selection:  All eligible entries will be entered into a random draw. The winner will be randomly selected from all eligible entries on 14/05/2020.
  1. Winner notification: The winner will be notified by Deliveroo's support agency Octagon, using any of the contact details linked to the winner's Deliveroo account provided at the time of entry. The winner will be contacted to confirm the win, and to arrange delivery of the Prize.
  1. Deliveroo will make reasonable efforts to contact the winner, but it is the winner's responsibility to monitor their voicemail and email account, including any junk mail folder, for receipt of the notification.
  1. Claiming the Prize: The winner must accept the Prize by confirming their eligibility and acceptance within 24 hours of the date of the winner's initial notification. Alternative winners may be selected from remaining competition entrants on 18/05/2020 and notified as set out above and must claim the Prize within  24 hours once notified.
  1. Prize delivery/collection: 
  1. Deliveroo's ticketing agency (Octagon) will contact winners to organise delivery of the Prize following acceptance of the Prize. It is the winner's responsibility to be available to collect the Prize at the time agreed with the winner.

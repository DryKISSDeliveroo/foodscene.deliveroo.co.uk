Free meal vouchers for NHS staff

  • 15 April 2020
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
terms
Share this article

Due to the popularity of Deliveroo vouchers, over 50,000 have now been claimed by NHS workers. We hope to make more available in the future.

  1. Deliveroo are providing 50,000 free £20 meal vouchers (a "Voucher") to NHS staff (the "Offer").
  2. How to claim:

All you need is a Deliveroo account linked to your NHS or HSC email address.

  • a. New to Deliveroo or existing account without an NHS email? Sign up to Deliveroo with your NHS email address.
  • b. Existing account with NHS email? This will be sent to you automatically.
  1. Receiving your Voucher: You will receive an email with your Voucher shortly after you have claimed the Offer.
  2. Redeeming your Voucher: Please insert your Voucher code in the Deliveroo app or on our website at the checkout. The Voucher can be used towards the costs of your meal and any Deliveroo delivery and service fees. If your order costs more than £20, you will be liable for the full remaining balance of order which shall be charged to the payment method selected. If your order costs less than £20, the remaining balance will be cancelled. Your Voucher must be used by 15/06/2020.
  3. Terms and Conditions:
  • a. This Offer is open to all NHS staff in the United Kingdom (including Northern Ireland) aged 18 years or over who have a Deliveroo account linked with a valid NHS email address.
  • b. You must have a valid NHS email address ending in: @nhs.net; @***.nhs.uk; @hscni.net; @***.hscni.net; @scot.nhs.net; and @wales.nhs.et.
  • c. One (1) Voucher is available per person. Voucher codes are unique and can only be used once. Each Voucher is non-transferable, non-exchangeable and no cash alternative is available.
  • d. This Offer will be valid from 14/04/2020 until the earlier of (i) 31/05/2020 or (ii) until 50,000 Vouchers have been claimed.
  • e. The Offer cannot be combined with any other offers or promotions. The Offer does not apply to business profiles.
  • f. The Offer will only apply where Deliveroo is available. Please check our website or app for availability of deliveries.
  • g. Deliveroo retains the right to amend, suspend or cancel this Offer at its discretion without notice.
  1. Personal data: Your personal data processed in connection with this Offer will be processed in accordance with the Deliveroo Privacy Notice which you can find at https://deliveroo.co.uk/privacy.
  2. Contact us: Please contact us at  if you need any help.

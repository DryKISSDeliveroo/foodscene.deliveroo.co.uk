Deliveroo & Mastercard - Win UEFA Champions League Final Tickets, 10 April 2017

  • 10 April 2017
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 8 min
Share this article

Eligibility: to enter the Deliveroo UEFA Champions League Final Cardiff 2017 promotion ("Promotion") participants must be 18 years or over, holders of a valid Mastercard and resident in England, Scotland, or Wales.  Residents of Northern Ireland and Republic of Ireland  and employees, officers and workers (or their family members) of the Promoter (as defined below), Mastercard, and affiliates of the Promoter or Mastercard may not enter this Promotion.  

  1. These terms and conditions ("Terms") apply to the Promotion.  By taking part in this Promotion all entrants will be deemed to have accepted and be bound by these Terms.
  2.  Promotional Period: the Promotion will run from 00:00 (GMT) on Monday 10 April 2017 until 23:59 (GMT) on Sunday 21 May 2017.
  3. In order to have your name include in the draw for the Promotion, participants must be an eligible participant and complete a Qualifying Transaction.
  4. For the purpose of these Terms and Conditions, a "Qualifying Transaction" means the placing of a fulfilled Deliveroo order, of any value, using a credit card or debit card that has been duly issued and authorised as a Mastercard (a "Mastercard") during the Promotional Period.
  5. A Qualifying Transaction equals one entry to the Promotion. Multiple Qualifying Transactions are permitted per participant and shall be entered into the draw.
  6. Prize: (i) a pair of tickets to the UEFA Champions League Final Cardiff 2017 to be held at the Principality Football Stadium in Cardiff, Wales on 3rd June 2017 ("Event"); (ii) one night's accommodation in a double or twin room at a hotel to be selected by the Promoter (at a value of £120, including VAT); and (iii) spending allowance of £500 to be put towards transport, meals, drinks, and tips for the duration of the trip ("Spending Allowance"). The total value of prize £1,170.
  7. The Prize is as stated and nothing else is included. The Winner (and guest) is responsible for all travel and other related expenses not forming part of the Prize, including but not limited to transport to travel insurance, and any transport, meal or other costs in excess of the Spending Allowance.
  8. There is no cash or other alternative to the Prize, in whole or in part.  The Prize is not transferable. The Promoter reserves the right to reclaim the Prize if it is transferred.
  9. Winner Selection: All valid entries received by the Promoter during the Promotional Period will be entered into a prize draw to be conducted within 5 working days of the end of the Promotional Period and, for your total confidence, the draw shall be conducted by PromoVeritas, the independent promotional verification service. The Judge's decision is final.
  10. Winner notification: The Winner will be notified via email and/or phone on or before Monday 29 May 2017. Winners have 24 hours to respond to initial notification ("Prize Notification") confirming they wish to claim the Prize. If the selected Winner does not respond within 24 hours from the Prize Notification, that Winner shall no longer be deemed eligible to claim the Prize and the PromoVeritas shall select an alternative Winner from random from the remaining valid entries.  That Winner shall have 24 hours to respond to the Prize Notification confirming they wish to claim the Prize. The Promoter and PromoVeritas and shall continue the selection process until a Winner has accepted the Prize.

Summary Terms and Conditions:

UK, 18+ only. 18/02/19 – 10/03/19. Post a photo of you sharing a KIND bar with a friend with the hashtag #KINDxDeliveroo the chance to select a charity to receive a £1000 donation. 10 winners selected via random prize draw. Charity must be selected from an approved list. Max 1 entry per person. No purchase necessary. Internet access required.

Promoter: KIND Snacks UK

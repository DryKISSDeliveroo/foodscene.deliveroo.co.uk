DELIVEROO PROMOTION RULES

These are the terms and conditions ("Rules") that apply to the Deliveroo Saturday Night Takeaway RooBus Promotion ("Promotion"). By entering the Promotion, each entrant agrees to these Rules and confirms they have read and understood the Deliveroo Privacy Notice at https://deliveroo.co.uk/privacy.

  1. Promoter: Roofoods Limited (trading as Deliveroo), a company incorporated and registered in England and Wales with registered company number 08167130 and registered office address at River Building, Level 1 Cannon Bridge House, 1 Cousin Lane, London EC4R 3TE  ("Deliveroo").
  1. Entry period: The Promotion opens for entry at 11:30 am (UK time) on 22/02/2020 and closes at 23:59 pm (UK time) on 21/03/2020 ("Entry Period").
  1. Eligibility: 
  • a. To participate in the Promotion, entrants must be a legal resident of England, Wales and Scotland. Residents of the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland are excluded.
  • b. Participants must be at least 18 years old at the time of entry to win a Prize. Participants under the age of 18 may complete the RooBus escape room, but will not be eligible to win a Prize.
  • c. A limit of one (1) entry per person applies to this Promotion.
  • d. A limit of one (1) Prize per person applies to this Promotion.
  • e. Deliveroo reserves the right at any time to require proof of eligibility in such form as Deliveroo reasonably considers necessary.

        

Not eligible: The following persons are not eligible to enter the Promotion: (i) any employee or other individual engaged by Deliveroo or any of its associated companies; (ii) members of the immediate family and/or household (whether related or not) of any of those individuals; and (iii) anyone else professionally involved with the operation or administration of the Promotion.

  1. How to enter: To enter the Promotion, entrants must complete the following steps between 11:30 am and 16:30 pm on a Saturday during the Entry Period, specifically 22/02/2020, 29/02/2020, 07/03/2020, 14/03/2020 and 21/03/2020 during the Entry Period (each, an "Entry Date"):
  • a. Enter the RooBus and complete the escape room on board by solving clues in four rooms to escape the top deck of the bus in the quickest time possible; and
  • b. Give your contact details to the RooBus operators.
  1. Prizes: There are 5 (five) prizes to be won in this Promotion ("Prizes"). Each Prize consists of:
  • a. a pair of tickets for the winner and one guest (aged 18 or over) to a live filming of Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway at BBC Studio White City ("Event"). Each Event shall take place on the Saturday immediately following the applicable Entry Date, specifically 29/02/2020, 07/03/2020, 14/03/2020, 21/03/2020 and 28/03/2020 (each, an "Event Date"); and
  • b. travel and accommodation, including:
  • i. Standard class return rail travel for two from the UK mainland station nearest to the winner's home address to London;
  • ii. Return private transfers for two between the arrival London station and the hotel in London;
  • iii. One (1) nights' accommodation for two at a 4* hotel in London on a bed and breakfast basis, based on the winner and their companion sharing a double/twin room (subject to availability); and
  • iv. Return private transfers for two between the hotel and BBC Studio White City on the night of the live recording; and
  • c. a VIP experience at the Event, including canapes and private bar.

Each person that completes the RooBus escape room shall be entitled to a food sample from a selected Deliveroo restaurant partner.

  1. Prize conditions: The following conditions apply to the Prize:
  • a. Each Prize is non-transferable, non-refundable, non-exchangeable and no cash alternative is available. However, if Deliveroo considers it appropriate, or if it becomes necessary for reasons beyond Deliveroo's control, a Prize may be varied or swapped for a suitable alternative in Deliveroo's discretion.
  • b. The Prize excludes transfers between the winner's home and the train station/airport, drinks, meals, additional transfers, excursions, attractions, spa treatments, gratuities, personal expenses, travel insurance and all other costs and arrangements not expressly included in clause 5 above.
  • c. Each Prize is subject to the terms and conditions of the ticket provider and the additional terms and conditions of Element London Limited ("Prize Provider"), both of which shall be provided to each winner upon acceptance of the Prize. The winner should ensure they are aware of any applicable expiry dates.
  • d. A Prize can only be won by one person. If two or more people completed the RooBus escape room together, the participants must decide who will be the representative of their group and provide their representatives' contact details to the RooBus operators. In the event that such a group completes the escape room in the quickest time and wins a Prize, the representative shall be the winner. For the avoidance of doubt, the Prize Provider will only contact the winner and not any other members of the group in the event that a Prize is won.  
  • e. It is the winner's responsibility to be available for the Event on the relevant Event Date.  
  • f. The winner and their companion will need a current and valid passport or driving licence for identification purposes, and will be required to show such passport or driving licence at the hotel, the Event and during travel.
  • g. Deliveroo will not provide any subsequent option for winner's who can no longer attend the Event on the relevant Event Date.
  • h. Either the winner or their companion will need a current and valid credit/debit card in order to check in at the hotel for security deposit purposes. It is the responsibility of the winner to ensure that they and/or their companions adhere to the local laws in the UK.
  • i. It is the responsibility of the winner to inform the Prize Provider of any wheelchair or similar access requirements for the winner and their companion when taking the Prize, and to adhere to any applicable health and safety guidelines.
  • j. All elements of the Prize must be taken at the same time. Once booked, the prize cannot be altered or amended.
  • k. The Prize and all its elements are subject to availability. Neither Deliveroo nor the Prize Provider will accept responsibility for the hotel or any facilities / elements of the Prize being unavailable, withdrawn or amended.
  1. Winner selection:  Each entrant is entitled to attempt to complete the RooBus escape room once. The time each entrant takes to complete the RooBus escape room will be recorded in minutes, seconds and milliseconds. The entrant that completes the RooBus escape room in the quickest amount of time in respect of that Entry Date shall win a Prize. In the event that two or more entrants have the same winning time, Deliveroo shall select the winner randomly. The winner shall be identified or selected by 18:00 pm on the relevant Entry Date.  
  1. Winner notification: The winner will be notified by the Prize Provider using the contact details provided at the time of entry on the first working day following the winner selection. The Prize Provider will make reasonable efforts to contact the winner, but it is the winner's responsibility to monitor their voicemail and email account, including any junk mail folder, for receipt of the notification.
  1. Claiming the Prize: The winner must accept the Prize by confirming their eligibility, acceptance and the full names and details of those attending the Event within 24 hours of the date of the initial winner's notification. The Prize is time-sensitive and therefore failure to accept the Prize by the deadline may result in the Prize being forfeited and the selection of an alternative winner. The alternative winner will be the person with the next quickest RooBus escape room completion time. Alternative winners will be notified as set out above and must claim the Prize within 24 hours.
  1. Prize collection: The Prizes will be managed by the Prize Provider. The process on how to obtain your Prizes will be confirmed by the Prize Provider, following acceptance of the Prize.

Background

  1. The promoter is Roofoods Ltd [(trading as Deliveroo)] (company number 08167130), whose registered office is at The River Building, Level 1 Cannon Bridge House, 1 Cousin Lane, London, United Kingdom, EC4R 3TE (the "Promoter").
  2. The competition is open to residents of the United Kingdom [(excluding Northern Ireland)] aged 18 and over at the date on which they enter the competition, except for employees or directors of the Promoter and its group companies, their immediate families or anyone else professionally connected with this competition. Where the prize (see the Prizes section below) involves the supply of any meals from partner restaurants, the competition is only open to individuals whose delivery address is within the Promoter's prescribed delivery area as listed on the Promoter's website or applications.

How to enter the competition

  1. For a chance of winning, tag your mum and her favourite dish on Facebook or Twitter and you will automatically be entered into the competition to win the prize (see the Prizes section below).  
  2. Only one entry per person is permitted.
  3. Entries must be made between 00:01 and 23:59 on 31 March 2018. Any entries after 23:59 on 31 March 2018 will not be entered into the competition.

Prizes 

  1. The winner will receive a £100 voucher code to spend on food and drink on the Deliveroo website or application. The voucher excludes delivery and service fees.  Applicable delivery and service fees must be paid by the winner. The winner will have 30 days from receipt of the voucher to apply to voucher to their account. Once the voucher has been applied to the account, the winner will be given 3 months to redeem the credit against eligible orders. Deliveroo Voucher and Account Credit Terms and Conditions apply.
  2. The prize is non-transferable and non-negotiable and there is no cash alternative.

Winners

  1. All entries received before 23:59 on 31 March 2018 will be entered into a prize draw and one winner will be selected at random. The winner will be informed of their selection on 1st April 2018 by private direct message on Twitter or Facebook. All costs and expenses not included within the prizes are the responsibility of the winner.
  2. If the winner does not respond to the Promoter within 14 days of notification, the Promoter reserves the right to select another winner using the process outlined above.
  3. The prize will be sent to the winner by private direct message on Facebook or Twitter within 2 days of the date on which the winner responds to the notification of their win.
  4. The winner will be required to participate in all reasonable promotional activity (such as publicity and photography).  By entering the competition, you consent to the Promoter using your name and photograph in promotional material.

  4. Winners may be subject to a verification process by an independent third party.

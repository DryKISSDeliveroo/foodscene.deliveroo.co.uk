Best in Category: First Round of Voting (Shortlist)

  • Members of the public will vote between 12:00 UTC 14 September 2021 and 23:59 27 September 2021 (the "First Voting Period") for their favourite Eligible Restaurants in each category to determine a shortlist of six Eligible Restaurants.
  • Only one vote per member of the public per Category in the First Voting Period will be counted. Where there is more than one vote in respect of a given Category from the same member of the public, only the first vote will be counted.
  • The six Eligible Restaurants per Category achieving the most votes in the First Voting Period, will be entered into the Second Round of Voting in respect of such Category ("Shortlisted").
  • Notwithstanding the above, an Eligible Restaurant may only be Shortlisted in respect of one Category. An Eligible Restaurant shall accordingly only be Shortlisted in respect of the Category for which it receives the most votes and its votes in respect of any other Category shall be excluded for the purposes of determining the Eligible Restaurants to be Shortlisted in that Category and for the purposes of the Second Round of Voting. If an Eligible Restaurant receives an equal amount of votes for multiple categories and could be Shortlisted for such multiple Categories, Deliveroo shall determine which Category the Eligible Restaurant is Shortlisted for.
  • In the event of a tie between multiple Eligible Restaurants, Deliveroo may elect to either a) Shortlist multiple Eligible Restaurants such that more than six Eligible Restaurants are entered into the Second Round of Voting, b) extend the First Voting Period, or c) seek determination from an independent adjudicator.

Best in Category: Second Round of voting

  • Members of the public will vote between 00:00 UTC 4 October 2021 and 23:59 18 October 2021(the "Second Voting Period") for their favourite Eligible Restaurants in each category to determine the winner in respect of such Category.
  • Only one vote per member of the public per Category in the Second Voting Period will be counted.
  • The Shortlisted Eligible Restaurant achieving the most votes in aggregate across both the First Voting Period and the Second Voting Period in each Category will be the winner of the Best Restaurant Award for such Category. In the event of a tie between multiple Eligible Restaurants, Deliveroo may elect to either award the Best Restaurant Award for such category to multiple Eligible Restaurants, extend the Second Voting Period, or seek determination from an independent adjudicator.
  • The winner of the Best Restaurant Award for each Category will be announced at the Deliveroo Restaurant of the Year Awards 2021 which shall take place no later than 31 December 2021.

DELIVEROO RESTAURANT AWARDS 2021 - RESTAURANT OF THE YEAR - TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Eligibility Criteria

To participate in the Deliveroo Restaurant Awards 2021 - Restaurant of the Year, restaurants must meet the following criteria (the "Eligibility Criteria"):

  • Restaurants must be available on the Deliveroo platform at the time of voting and at the time that the awards are announced;
  • Restaurant must not be in any dispute with, or subject to investigation by, Deliveroo or any regulatory authority, or otherwise, in Deliveroo's reasonable opinion, be likely to bring Deliveroo or the Deliveroo Restaurant Awards into disrepute.
  • Restaurants must have a branch located in the UK and Ireland.
  • Restaurants otherwise agree to and comply with, the terms of herein.
  • Each restaurant meeting the Eligibility Criteria will be an "Eligible Restaurant". Deliveroo, acting in its sole discretion, will determine whether Eligibility Criteria have been met.

Restaurant of the Year: Public Vote

  • Members of the public will vote between 12:00 UK time 13 September 2021 and 23:59 UK time 27 September 2021 (the "Public Vote") for their favourite Eligible Restaurants as the Restaurant of the Year to determine a shortlist of six Eligible Restaurants.
  • Only one vote per member of the public in the Public Vote will be counted. Where there is more than one vote from the same member of the public, only the first vote will be counted. Â 
  • The 6 Eligible Restaurants achieving the most votes in the Public Vote will be shortlisted for the final ("Shortlisted"). In the event of a tie between multiple Eligible Restaurants, Deliveroo may elect to either a) Shortlist multiple Eligible Restaurants such that more than six Eligible Restaurants are entered into the Judging phase b) extend the Public Vote, or c) seek determination from an independent adjudicator.
  • The judging panel will review the six Shortlisted Eligible Restaurants (who fit this criteria) with the most votes and select the winner

Restaurant of the Year: Judging

  • The 6 Shortlisted Eligible Restaurants will be submitted to the panel of judges, who will, together, determine the Restaurant of the Year on the basis of the following criteria:
  • Going the extra mile for customers
  • Providing value for money
  • Brand personality
  • Quality of food
  • Packaging and presentation
  • Customer reviews
  • The panel of judges ("Judging Panel")will consist of:
  • Will Shu, Founder and CEO of Deliveroo; and
  • Three other independent adjudicators
  • The Eligible Restaurant selected by the Judging Panel as best meeting the criteria above in aggregate will be the Deliveroo Restaurant Awards 2021 Restaurant of the Year. The Judging Panel's decision is final.
  • The winner of the Deliveroo Restaurant Awards 2021 Restaurant of the Year Award will be announced at the Deliveroo Restaurant of the Year Awards 2021 which shall take place no later than 31 December 2021

DELIVEROO PROMOTION RULES

These are the terms and conditions ("Rules") that apply to the Deliveroo Emirates FA Cup Fifth Round - FA Quarter Final Ticket and Deliveroo Credit Giveaway Promotion ("Promotion"). By entering the Promotion, each entrant agrees to these Rules and confirms they have read and understood the Deliveroo Privacy Notice at https://deliveroo.co.uk/privacy.

  1. Promoter: Roofoods Limited (trading as Deliveroo), a company incorporated and registered in England and Wales with registered company number 08167130 and registered office address at River Building, Level 1 Cannon Bridge House, 1 Cousin Lane, London EC4R 3TE  ("Deliveroo").
  1. Entry Periods: There are two entry periods:

Entry Period One opens for entry at 12:01 am (UK time) on 02/03/2020 and closes at 11:59 pm (UK time) on 05/03/2020

Entry Period Two opens for entry at 12:01 am (UK time) on 07/03/2020 and closes at 11:59 pm (UK time) on 08/03/2020

(together, the "Entry Periods")

  1. Eligibility: 
  1. To participate in the Promotion, entrants must be a legal resident of England, Scotland and Wales and at least 18 years old at the time of entry. Residents or Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland are excluded.
  2. Internet access and a Deliveroo account are required to enter the Promotion.
  3. Purchase is necessary to enter.
  4. Deliveroo reserves the right at any time to require proof of eligibility in such form as Deliveroo reasonably considers necessary.

Not eligible: The following persons are not eligible to enter the Promotion: (i) any employee or other individual engaged by Deliveroo or any of its associated companies; (ii) members of the immediate family and/or household (whether related or not) of any of those individuals; and (iii) anyone else professionally involved with the operation or administration of the Promotion.

  1. How to enter: To enter the Promotion, entrants must complete the following steps during the Entry Periods:
  1. Log into the entrant's Deliveroo account on www.deliveroo.co.uk or the Deliveroo app; and
  2. enter one of the following ten promotion codes (each a "Promotion Code", together the "Promotion Codes") BRFA5WIN, CRMFA5WIN, IAFA5WIN, PSFA5WIN, PSFA5TWIN, PSFA5CWIN, THEFACUP, FLFAWIN, EXFA5WIN or UNIFA5WIN, before placing a Qualifying Order;

A "Qualifying Order" means a paid-for and delivered Deliveroo order of a minimum value of £10.00 (excluding tips, delivery charges and other service-related charges) during an Entry Period);

Each Qualifying Order made with a Promotion Code gives an entrant one entry into the prize draw.

  1. Prizes: There are 28 (twenty eight) prizes (each a "Prize" and together the "Prizes") to be won in this Promotion, consisting of 20 (twenty) pairs of tickets to an Emirates FA Cup Quarter Finals match (each an "Event" and together the "Events") and  8 (eight) £150 Deliveroo credit vouchers. Locations of the Events and the number of tickets for each Event shall be confirmed following the completion of the 5th Round fixtures and Quarter Final Draw. Fixtures and seats will be selected by Deliveroo acting in its sole discretion. Event tickets are subject to availability and will be allocated to winners on a first come first serve basis at the discretion of Deliveroo.

  1. Prize winners and their guests are responsible for all travel and other costs related to attending the Event. Deliveroo will not provide transport or other costs. It is the responsibility of the Prize winners to be available for the requested date for the Event. Deliveroo will not provide any subsequent option to Prize winners customers who can no longer attend the Event on dates requested.
  1. The prize winner and their guest must be aged 18 or over.
  1. Only one prize may be won per person.
  1. Each Prize is non-transferable, non-refundable, non-exchangeable and no cash alternative is available. However, if Deliveroo considers it appropriate, or if it becomes necessary for reasons beyond Deliveroo's control, a Prize may be varied or swapped for a suitable alternative in Deliveroo's discretion.
  1. Each Prize is subject to Deliveroo's Voucher Terms and Conditions or the terms and conditions of the ticket provider, which can be found at [url]. The winner should ensure they are aware of any applicable expiry dates.
  1. Winner selection:  All eligible entries will be entered into a random draw. The winner will be randomly selected from all eligible entries on 09/03/2020.
  1. Winner notification: The winner will be notified by Deliveroo's support agency Octagon, using any of the contact details linked to the winner's Deliveroo account provided at the time of entry. Depending on the prize won, winners will be contacted to discuss ticket availability for the Events or be  provided with winning Deliveroo credit codes.
  1. Deliveroo will make reasonable efforts to contact the winner, but it is the winner's responsibility to monitor their voicemail and email account, including any junk mail folder, for receipt of the notification.
  1. Claiming the Prize: The winner must accept the Prize by confirming their eligibility and acceptance within 48 hours of the date of the initial winner's notification. The Prize is time-sensitive and therefore failure to accept the Prize by the deadline may result in the Prize being forfeited and, at Deliveroo's sole discretion, the selection of an alternative winner. Alternative winners may be selected from remaining competition entrants on 11/03/2020 and notified as set out above and must claim the Prize within  24 hours once notified.
  1. Prize delivery/collection: 
  1. Event tickets: The Event tickets will be managed by Deliveroo's ticketing agency (Octagon). The process on how to obtain your tickets will be confirmed by Octagon, following acceptance of the Prize.
  2. Winners of Deliveroo credit: This Prize will be credited to the winner's Deliveroo account within five (5) working days of the date of the winner's notification.

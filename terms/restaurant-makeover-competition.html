  1. The competition
  1. The title of the competition is Win a makeover for your restaurant up to the value of £25,000.
  2. All you need to do is apply at restaurants.deliveroo.com/perks/restaurantmakeover and we'll get in contact with you if you are successful.
  1. How to enter
  1. The competition will run from 09.00 on May 30 (the Opening Date) to 09.00 on June 15 2019 (the Closing Date) inclusive.
  2. All competition entries must be received electronically by the Promoter by no later than 09.00 on the Closing Date. All competition entries received after the Closing Date are automatically disqualified.
  3. To enter the competition simply answer each question in the entry form found at restaurants.deliveroo.com/perks/restaurantmakeover by the Closing Date.

The Promoter will not accept responsibility for competition entries that are lost, mislaid, damaged or delayed in transit, regardless of cause, including, for example, as a result of any technical malfunction, systems, satellite, network, server, computer hardware or software failure of any kind.

  1. Eligibility
  1. The competition is only open to Deliveroo restaurant partners in UK, except restaurant partners owned or operated by:
  1. employees of the Promoter or its holding or subsidiary companies;
  2. employees of agents or suppliers of the Promoter or its holding or subsidiary companies, who are professionally connected with the competition or its administration; or
  3. members of the immediate families or households of (a) and (b) above.
  1. In entering the competition, you confirm that you are eligible to do so. The Promoter may require you to provide proof that you are eligible to enter the competition.
  2. There is a limit of one entry per restaurant partner.
  3. The Promoter reserves all rights to disqualify you if your conduct is contrary to the spirit or intention of the prize competition.
  1. The prize
  1. The winners of the competition will have their restaurant refurbished by the Promoter alongside its procurement partner up to a value of £25,000.  
  2. The prize will include labour, materials, design and associated professional fees up to a maximum total value of £25,000 per winner.  The prize is subject to any necessary approvals or consents, including landlord approvals, which must be obtained by the winners. The prize excludes any works that require planning permission.  The prize is subject to the winners entering a written agreement with the Promoter detailing the refurbishment works to be carried out, including timescales for completion.  The winners must be willing to close their restaurant while the refurbishment works are carried out for a reasonable period to ensure compliance with relevant food hygiene and health and safety requirements.  The Promoter will not be responsible for ongoing maintenance after the refurbishment is completed.  
  3. The prize is non-transferable and there is no cash alternative.
  1. Winners
  1. The competition entries will be judged by a panel of three judges. The panel shall select a shortlist of finalists. The decision of the panel of judges (acting reasonably) will be final.
  2. Three winners will be selected from the shortlist of finalists by public vote.
  3. The Promoter will contact the winners personally as soon as practicable after the Announcement Date, using the telephone number or email address provided with the competition entry.
  4. The winners of the competition will be announced on social media by 30 September 2019 (Announcement Date).
  5. The winners will be required to participate in all reasonable promotional activity (such as publicity and photography).  In particular, by entering the competition, you consent to the Promoter using your name and photograph in promotional material.
  6. The name and county of each winner can be obtained by sending a stamped addressed envelope marked "Win a makeover for your restaurant Competition Winner" to the Promoter's address.
  1. Claiming the prize
  1. The Promoter will make all reasonable efforts to contact the winners. If the winners cannot be contacted or are not available, or have not claimed their prize within 14 days of the Announcement Date, the Promoter reserves the right to offer the prize to the next eligible entrant selected from the shortlist of finalists.
  2. The Promoter does not accept any responsibility if you are not able to take up the prize.

Your statutory rights are not affected.

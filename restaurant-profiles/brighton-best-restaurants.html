The best of Brighton: ten of the tastiest restaurants

  • 1 May 2017
  • Emma Austin
  • Read time: 4 min
brighton restaurants ideas vegan street food
Share this article

Brighton – there are more blue plaques in this colourful coastal town than you can shake a stick at. From Sir Winston Churchill to Fatboy Slim, countless household names have called Brighton and Hove home at one time or another.

But it's not just the familiar faces that define this vibrant resort; the streets are famous for their unique restaurants, quirky cafés and bakeries that are bound to delight any local or passing day-tripper.

To take you through some of this seaside town's most awesome eats, we've made this handy guide to the best restaurants in Brighton that you can find right here on Deliveroo.

The Chilli Pickle

The dishes at this hugely popular Indian restaurant are as bold and brilliant as Brighton itself. Inspired by his travels in India, Head Chef and owner Alun Sperring has earned countless awards and accolades, including Best Takeaway for two years on the trot at the British Curry Awards. You might also recognise him from BBC's The Best British Takeaways. For the full Chilli Pickle experience, order the punters' favourite on Deliveroo, the Grand Thali Box.

La Choza

A post shared by Aoife Sweeney (@lachozamexican) on Mar 16, 2017 at 2:05pm PDT

La Choza is turning coastal cuisine on its head with fiery Mexican street food. Swap soggy fish and chips for tasty tacos and mighty burritos from yet another of Brighton's award-winning restaurants. La Choza scooped the Just as Good at Home prize at the BRAVO awards this year, and you can try some their triumphant tostadas right here on Deliveroo.

VIP Very Italian Pizza

A post shared by #VeryItalianPizza (@v.i.p.veryitalianpizza) on Jan 18, 2017 at 1:34pm PST

With a history that dates back to 1845 in Naples, this family-run pizza business has certainly earned its moniker. It's one of the top ten restaurants on Deliveroo in Brighton, and for good reason. They've stuck to their family roots by topping their pies with fior di latte mozzarella from their own farm and the godfather of the tomato, the San Marzano. Bring a taste of Naples home to Brighton, here.

Purezza

A post shared by Purezza (@purezzauk) on Aug 8, 2016 at 5:44am PDT

Great minds put man on the moon, and now Purezza has pulled off the guilt-free pizza. Vegans and pizza lovers avoiding gluten can rejoice, as Purezza piles their pies high with totally animal-free toppings that don't scrimp on flavour. For a pious, plant-based pizza, check out Purezza on Deliveroo.

Curry Leaf Café – Kemptown Kitchen

A post shared by Curry Leaf Cafe (@curry_leaf_cafe) on Mar 27, 2017 at 11:20am PDT

Curry Leaf Café – Kemptown Kitchen puts a modern spin on flavoursome Indian street food. Their small plates, created with locally sourced ingredients, are designed to be shared. We first featured these curry connoisseurs at our Christmas pop-up and now you can order a Curry Leaf Café feast direct from Deliveroo.

Moshimo

Sleek, stylish and sustainably sourced, Moshimo ticks all the boxes for fresh, Instagram-friendly Japanese dishes. Whether you're in the mood for delicately crafted sushi or a warming chicken katsu, Moshimo are your go-to on Deliveroo in Brighton.

Lovefit Café

Lovefit Café is a protein lover's paradise. If you're looking to maintain your lean machine physique, then their super meaty Surfer Burgers and tasty smoothies are the perfect fuel for a day out in the Brighton surf.

Happy Maki

A post shared by Happy Maki (@thehappymaki) on Dec 15, 2016 at 3:49am PST

Happy Maki's creations might come minus meat and fish but these veggie sushi wraps deliver full-on flavour, with combinations like crispy mock hoisin duck with avocado and sweet potato with roasted coconut. For sushi without the fins, why not order in?

Doughbo

A post shared by doughbo (@doughbo_info) on Aug 14, 2016 at 5:23am PDT

Two friends and a few beers were the brains behind Doughbo. These gourmet doughballs have built up quite the cult following in Brighton, and with oozy, woodfired sweet and savoury treats on the menu, it's easy to see why. Check them out on Deliveroo.

Glazed

A post shared by Glazed (@glazed_coffee) on Nov 1, 2016 at 2:58am PDT

For indulgent donuts without the dairy, check out the selection on offer at Glazed. Brighton does vegan cuisine in a big way and these guys add some fun-filled additions to the town's animal-free food scene. With sticky glazes and cute sprinkles, these are our kind of "holefoods".

Whether it's vegan treats or a street food feast that you've got a hankering for, you can order the very best of Brighton on Deliveroo.

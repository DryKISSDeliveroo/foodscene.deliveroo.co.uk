A fiesta of Mexican feasts

  • 30 October 2017
  • Andy Nolan
  • Read time: 3 min
deliveroo mexican delivery
Share this article

Light, but packed full of fresh and spicy flavours, Mexican cuisine really brings the party to your plate. To celebrate Mexican week, we've put together a list of the best burritos, top tacos and fiery fajitas for you to enjoy.

Benito's Hat (London)

A post shared by Belt & Braces PR (@beltandbracespr) on Oct 11, 2017 at 7:24am PDT

Named after former Mexican president Benito Juarez, and his collection of unusual hats, you have to tip your cap to these burrito bosses. The fun doesn't stop with burritos though, they've also got top quality quesadillas and simple, fresh salads to transport your taste buds to sunny Mexico.

Chiquito (Nationwide)

A post shared by Chiquito Restaurant (@the_chiquito) on Sep 12, 2017 at 5:34am PDT

For the best of everything between Texas and Acapulco, Chiquito should be your number one pick. From Mexican paella to Southern Fried Chicken, from burgers to burritos, Chiquitos is a celebration of all things Tex Mex that you've been missing!

Tortilla (Nationwide)

A post shared by Tortilla (@tortillauk) on Jul 30, 2017 at 11:17am PDT

Ever since this Californian husband-and-wife team opened Tortilla's doors for the first time, their mission was simple: whether it's a grab and go lunch or a leisurely meal with a few beers, they want to give you the authentic Mexican taste experience.

Taqueria (London)

A post shared by Taqueria London (@taqueriauk) on Oct 16, 2017 at 10:39am PDT

Having started out life as everyone's favourite Mexican food stall on Portobello Road, Taqueria's selection of fresh tacos, tasty tostadas and quesadillas can't be beaten. Using only the very best ingredients, Taqueria is an absolute must-try.

Boojum (Belfast, Cork, Galway and Dublin)

A post shared by Boojum (@boojummex) on Aug 11, 2017 at 9:22am PDT

To paraphrase Bill Shankly, "Burritos aren't a matter of life and death, they're much more important than that." If this sentence speaks to you then you need to get yourself to Boojum – no burrito place inspires unwavering loyalty quite like these guys. Enjoy pilgrims.

Taco Bell (Sheffield, Nottingham and Leeds)

A post shared by Taco Bell UK (@tacobelluk) on Jul 10, 2017 at 1:09pm PDT

This is not a drill! The taco tycoons from across the pond have landed, and they've brought their famous tacos, nachos, burritos and quesadillas with them. Finally, you don't have to look at an old Sir Mixalot video to get closer to the action.

Lucha Libre (Liverpool)

A post shared by Lucha Libre (@luchaliverpool) on Oct 21, 2017 at 5:31am PDT

A tribute to the food you would find in the restaurants and on the streets of Guadalajara, Lucha Libre serves up all of your favourite no-frills Mexican flavours. If you're feeling like something more North of the border, then check out their inspired burgers and dirty fries .

Mildred's (London)

A post shared by VEGAN🌱AIP🌻GF🌾 (@vegan_aip_gf) on Oct 17, 2017 at 3:34am PDT

Whether you're vegan one day a week or you live that clean life every day, Mildred's have something for you. Mexican dishes might be stereotypically meaty, but why don't you let Mildred's guac style smashed avocado change your mind? Maybe a mouth-watering meat-free burrito next?

Dine with Banana Tree, get a free juice with every main

  • 17 July 2018
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 1 min
promotion
Share this article

As a Plus customer, we want to reward you with the best food experiences. Sushi on the sofa, pizza parties at the office – and the joy of sitting down at a great restaurant.

That's why we're offering you a free juice with every main on your next visit to Banana Tree!

Here's how to enjoy your free juice:

  1. Visit Banana Tree in Angel, Bayswater, West Hampstead, Clapham, Maida Vale, Soho, Oxford, Milton Keynes, or Chelmsford before 23rd August 2018.
  1. Just make sure you've updated your app to the latest version then tap the Offers tab. You can find your personal Plus offers there. Show that screen to the restaurant.
  1. Get a free juice with every main (max 4 per table, available Monday-Thursday, excluding lunch.)

We hope you have a great time.

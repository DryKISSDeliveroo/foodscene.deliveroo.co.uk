DELIVEROO PROMOTION RULES

These are the terms and conditions ("Rules") that apply to the Pepsi Max UEFA Football Competition ("Promotion"). By entering the Promotion, each entrant agrees to these Rules and confirms they have read and understood the Deliveroo Privacy Notice at https://deliveroo.co.uk/privacy.

  1. Promoter: Roofoods Limited (trading as Deliveroo), a company incorporated and registered in England and Wales with registered company number 08167130 and registered office address at River Building, Level 1 Cannon Bridge House, 1 Cousin Lane, London EC4R 3TE  ("Deliveroo").
  1. Entry period: The Promotion opens for entry at 10:00am (UK time) on 12/05/2022 and closes at 11:59 pm (UK time) on 31/07/2022 ("Entry Period"). There will be an additional "wrap up draw" between 01/08/22 and 31/01/23.
  1. Eligibility: 
  1. To participate in the Promotion, entrants must be a legal resident of England, Scotland, Republic of Ireland or Wales and 18 years old at the time of entry. Residents of Northern Ireland are excluded. 
  2. A limit of two (2) entries per person, per day applies to this Promotion.
  3. Internet access and an active Deliveroo account are required to enter the Promotion.
  4. Deliveroo reserves the right at any time to require proof of eligibility in such form as Deliveroo reasonably considers necessary.
  1. Not eligible: The following persons are not eligible to enter the Promotion: (i) any employee or other individual engaged by Promoter or any of its associated companies; (ii) members of the immediate family and/or household (whether related or not) of any of those individuals; and (iii) anyone else professionally involved with the operation or administration of the Promotion.
  1. How to enter: To enter the Promotion, entrants must log into the entrant's Deliveroo account on www.deliveroo.co.uk or the Deliveroo app during the Entry Period and place a Qualifying Order. A "Qualifying Order" means a paid-for and delivered Deliveroo order which includes any Pepsi Max, Walkers Max crisps or any Doritos products.
  1. Scan the QR code on the back of the pack with a mobile camera or go to www.getmatchready.co.uk where the customer will need to enter their email address, full name, mobile number, preferred venue, product purchased, and confirm they are  over 18 years of age at the time of entry. ROI residents will be asked to successfully complete a game of skill.
  2. Enter the batch code from pack. On Walkers Max and Doritos packs the batch code can be found on the back of pack. On Pepsi Max the batch code can be found on the bottom of the can, or on the bottle neck for PET packs. See www.getmatchready.co.uk/faqs for further details.
  3. Please retain all packaging with any unique codes as this may be required for validation and in order for the winner to receive their Prize
  1. Prizes: There are three separate prize elements to be won in this Promotion ("Prize") . Main Promotion Ticket Prize Winner Selection: There are five hundred (500) pairs of tickets to a UEFA Women's Euro 2022 game. There is one (x1) cash prize of £10 (or Euro equivalent). Ticket prizes shall consist of 2 tickets to a UEFA Women's Euro 2022 game only and any travel, accommodation or out of pocket expenses are excluded.  Winners shall be required to download and keep the UEFA ticket mobile app, details of which will be provided in the email sent to winners at time of win. Tickets are issued subject to UEFA's ticketing terms and conditions which can be found here and UEFA's stadium rules which can be found here. Wrap up Draw Prize: Consists of one (x1) cash prize of £500 (or Euro equivalent) to be won in the "wrap up period". Max 1 x pair of tickets and 1 x cash prize in total per person. Maximum of 2 entries is permitted per person per day during the Promotion Period. Each purchase may only be used to enter once. Whilst we want customers to enjoy this promotion, we discourage over consumption. Please do not purchase more product than you can reasonably consume to increase your chances of winning.  Single prize pool across all promotional products.
  2. Prize conditions: The Prize is non-transferable, non-refundable, non-exchangeable and is not for resale. 
  1. Winner selection: All eligible entries will be entered into a draw and selected at random. Prizes will be available via "winning moments" (days, hours, minutes, seconds) set between 10:00 BST on 12 May 2022 and 23:59 BST on 16 July 2022. These moments have been randomly selected by an independent verification service. If an entrant's time of valid entry is the first to fall on or after a winning moment, they win the Prize randomly pre-allocated to that moment. If two entries are received at the same time, the entry processed by the server first will be the winning entry. Any Prizes not won before the next winning moment are rolled over and will be allocated to the winner in the next winning moment. Instant Win: Customers can find out instantly if they are a winner of the UEFA Women's Euro 2022 match tickets via scanning the QR code and entering their details. Tickets for each venue are limited. If tickets to the preferred venue are not available at time of win, winners will receive tickets to an alternative venue. Winners will also find out instantly if they are a cash prize winner. Winners will then receive an email with a secure link to claim.
  1. Winner notification:  Main Promotion Ticket Prize Winner Notification: Winners will be notified instantly on screen and redemption instructions will appear on screen. Winners can then choose a UEFA Women's Euro 2022 game for which they want tickets, subject to availability, and to confirm their email address to claim their Prize. If a winner does not follow the instructions at time of win, the Promoter reserves the right to disqualify that winner and return the Prizes to Prize pool which may be won by another entrant during the Promotion Period. Main Promotion Cash Winner Notification: Winners will be notified instantly on screen and will receive an email sent to the email address used at time of entry which contains a unique claim link that will allow the winner to claim their prize by providing full name, bank account number and sort code. Prizes to be claimed within 3 months of the date of win. Wrap up Draw Winner Notification: The winner will be contacted via the email address provided upon entry within 5 working days of the draw and will be required to respond to confirm identity, eligibility plus acceptance of the prize within 14 days of initial contact. In the event a winner does not respond to communications within 14 days of initial contact, the Promoter reserves the right to select an alternative winner from all remaining entries in the prize draw, with the originally selected winner forfeiting their right to the prize.
  1. Claiming the Prize: 
  1. In the event a winner does not respond to communications within 14 days of initial contact, the Promoter reserves the right to select an alternative winner from all remaining entries in the prize draw, with the originally selected winner forfeiting their right to the prize.
  1. Prize delivery:  Main promotion Ticket Prize Delivery: Ticket winners will be sent an email containing a link which will take them to UEFA's ticketing system where winners and their guests will be required to register and provide identification to claim their tickets. Following successful prize claim, e-tickets for two to a UEFA Women's Euro 2022 game will be sent to winners by email to the email address used at time of claim or delivered to the UEFA mobile app downloaded by the winner Prizes will usually be delivered within 2 days but in the unlikely event that a Prize doesn't arrive within 7 days of the winner claiming their Prize, winners will have a further 7 days to inform the Promoter by contacting us at getmatchready@responsehandlingteam.co.uk. In the event that a winner does not inform the Promoter within this timeframe, the Promoter will reserve the right to not reissue the prize or limit its value at their sole discretion. The Promoter will not be responsible for any incorrect email details provided by the winner. Main Promotion Cash Prize Delivery: All cash prize transfers are via BACS made by the Promoter's agency. Bank transfer is the only method of prize transfer available. Prizes will usually be delivered within 5 – 12 days but in the unlikely event that a Prize doesn't arrive within 60 days of the winner claiming their Prize, winners will have a further 28 days to inform the Promoter by contacting us at getmatchready@responsehandlingteam.co.uk. In the event that a winner does not inform the Promoter within this timeframe, the Promoter will reserve the right to not reissue the prize or limit its value at their sole discretion. The Promoter will not be responsible for any incorrect bank account details provided by the winner. Wrap up Draw Prize Delivery: If you receive a notification that you are the Wrap Up Draw Prize winner, you will be asked to provide postal address details and your bank account details to which your Prize will be sent within 28 days of confirming details. In the unlikely event that a Prize doesn't arrive within this timeframe, the winners will have a further 28 days to inform the Promoter by emailing getmatchready@responsehandlingteam.co.uk. If a winner does not do so, the Promoter reserves the right to not reissue the Prize or limit its value at its sole discretion.

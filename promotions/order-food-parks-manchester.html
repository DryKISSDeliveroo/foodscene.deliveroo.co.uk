Now delivering to parks in Manchester – get outside with Deliveroo

  • 24 May 2017
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 3 min
parks manchester picnic summer
Share this article

Summer is served. Get breakfast, lunch and drinks delivered to the park in Manchester. Here's where you can get all your Deliveroo favourites.

Leave the picnic hamper at home, because you can now order Deliveroo to your favourite suntraps in Manchester. If you're dining al fresco instead of al desko on your lunch break, or fancy chilling in the park at the weekend, all you need to do is grab a spot in the sun and we'll do the rest. Here's how to get Deliveroo outdoors this summer.

Here's how it works:

  1. Download or open the Deliveroo app for iOS or Android
  2. Enter your location
  3. Drop the delivery pin at the nearest park entrance
  4. Head over to a suggested meeting point once you get a delivery notification and meet your rider

Which parks will you deliver to?

All summer, we'll be helping you to make the most of the sunshine in these Manchester parks:

  • St John's Gardens
  • Didsbury Park
  • Platt Fields Park
  • Whitworth Park
  • Heaton Park

How will I find my rider?

When your order's ready, we'll send you a notification so you can go and meet your rider at one of our meeting points. We'll tell you which meeting point to go to, then all you need to do is pick up your food and drinks, then dig in. You'll find postcodes to each point below, with a few simple directions to help you out, so finding your rider should be a piece of cake.

St John's Gardens

West Meeting Point: M3 4AP, meet at the west park entrance, at the intersection of Great John St and Lower Byrom St.

East Meeting Point: M3 4DT, meet at the east park entrance, at the intersection of St John St and Byrom St.

Didsbury Park

West Meeting Point: M20 6WH, meet at the intersection of Gillbrook Rd and Knight St, in front of the Pavilion Café.

East Meeting Point: M20 5LR, meet at the intersection of Sandhurst Rd and Dalston Dr, right in front of the entrance of the park.

Platt Fields Park

Wilmslow Rd Meeting Point: M14 5JQ, meet at the corner of where Brighton Grove and Wilmslow Rd intersect, near the Abbey Solicitors car park.

Whitmore Rd Meeting Point: M14 7BH, meet at the west entrance, just off Whitmore Rd.

Car Park Meeting Point: M14 6LT, meet at the main entrance car park, just off Mabfield Rd.

Whitworth Park

South East Meeting Point: M14 5TP, meet at the south east entrance of the park, at the intersection of Moss Ln East and Oxford Rd, near a restaurant called Aldiwan Cafe (also known as Golden).

North West Meeting Point: M15 6FG, meet at the north west park entrance, near the car park at the intersection of Denmark Rd and Acomb St.

Heaton Park

North Car Park Meeting Point: M25 2GT, meet at Heaton Park car park, by the Heaton Park Reservoir, through the main Heaton Park gate entrance on St Margaret's Rd.

Heaton Park Tram Stop Meeting Point: M25 1JE, meet at the entrance of the park on Bury Old Rd, by the Heaton Park Tram Stop (C).

South Meeting Point: M25 0BL, meet at the main south entrance of the park on Sheepfoot Ln.

Please remember to help keep your parks tidy and dispose of your rubbish responsibly.

We've got your summer sorted! Order to the park now.

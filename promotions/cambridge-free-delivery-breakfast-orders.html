Cambridge: Get free delivery on breakfast orders this weekend

  • 28 April 2017
  • Editor
  • Read time: 3 min
cambridge breakfast free delivery
Share this article

We all love a Bank Holiday. The lie-ins, the chance to spend time with family, and the excuse to kick back and eat great food. But if breakfast in bed tickles your fancy, there's even more an excuse to indulge this weekend: we're offering free delivery before noon. Whether you want avocado toast with all the toppings or a cheeky pile of pancakes smothered in syrup, order in guilt-free because delivery's on us.

Free delivery in Cambridge

A post shared by Crepeaffaire (@crepeaffaire) on Jul 28, 2016 at 9:44am PDT

That's right, this Saturday and Sunday we're offering free delivery in Cambridge when you order breakfast.

Order a feast for your friends – or a little something for yourself – and take us up on the offer, just because. Between 9am and 12pm, see our standard delivery charge disappear. So get ready to dig through your favourite menus and make the decision of a lifetime – will your eggs be scrambled, poached or served sunny side up?

Win a £100 breakfast voucher

A post shared by Carluccio's (@carluccios) on Jan 29, 2017 at 2:59am PST

To top off our little treat, we're offering customers the chance to win a mega breakfast buffet. When you order, a £100 breakfast could be yours.

Starting this weekend and running for the next two weeks, a big ol' brekkie will be up for grabs. When you spot any 'Want us to bankroll your brunch' menu item on site, it's your time to shine. Hop on the aptly named 'Click here to enter' button and your work will be done.

So keep your eyes peeled. The button will be around on all local menus during breakfast hours this weekend.

Cambridge's brunch scene

A post shared by Nanna Mexico (@nannamexico) on Jan 14, 2017 at 2:30am PST

First the free delivery had you hooked and now the competition has sealed the deal. So let's talk options. Cambridge knows brunch. The eateries here make up one of the best breakfast scenes across the UK – you can trust us on that one. Eclectic, exciting and undeniably enticing, the diverse range of dishes across the Cambridge menu collection are yours for the choosing.

You've got your classic breakfast options of course, like poached eggs and salmon from Carluccio's, but you can also go for something with Mexican flair. Nanna Mexico's Egg & Chorizo Burrito is topped with cheese and served with salty tortilla chips for extra crunch.

Or maybe you're in the mood for a double helping of sweet and savoury? CrepeAffaire serve their classic French style crepes alongside all day breakfast specials topped with fluffy scrambled eggs, crispy bacon and all of the cheese.

To benefit from free delivery in Cambridge with your breakfast in bed this Saturday and Sunday, make your order with Deliveroo.

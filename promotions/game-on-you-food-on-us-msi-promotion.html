Game on you, food on us - Get up to £40 of Deliveroo vouchers

  • 12 November 2019
  • Deliveroo
  • Read time: 2 min
promotion
Share this article

You've just upgraded your setup and you're ready to settle in for an epic weekend-long gaming sesh - but disaster strikes. The cupboards are bare and the only thing left in the fridge is a bottle of out of date sour cream. Fear not, MSI have your back!

 From 12th November to 31st December 2019 (or while stocks lasts), MSI are running their 'Game on you, Food on us' campaign. With the purchase of selected MSI gaming products, customers will be able to redeem Deliveroo Gift cards worth up to £40 - ensuring there's plenty of fuel for the frags!

 For further information please visit: http://bit.ly/34R0U7T

 Participating retailers:

●      Box.co.uk

●      OCUK

●      Scan.co.uk

●      CCLOnline

●      Ebuyer

About MSI

 As a world leading gaming brand, MSI is the most trusted name in gaming and esports. We stand by our principles of breakthroughs in design, the pursuit of excellence, and technological innovation. Integrating games' most coveted extreme performance, realistic visuals, authentic sound, precise control and smooth streaming functions into its gaming rig. MSI frees gamers from tedious trial and error and pushes gaming performance beyond limits. The determination to surpass past achievements has made MSI a 'True Gaming' brand with gaming spirit throughout the industry!
